About what: [[德中學 Learning by Caring]]

used to dislike the cognitive, mindless, route cognitive approach to learning, as advocated by both [[Minerva]] and [[Young, Scott]]. But these two days I feel I get to resolute those two, by taking "learning by caring as 道," "ultralearning as 術." Most people only needs the 道, as it is axiologically good, or effective in the sense that it focuses on the right thing. Ultralearning, may only be needed by those who pursue the non-natural/artificial or aspire to be the top above everyone else, as it helps one achieve their goals more efficiently.
