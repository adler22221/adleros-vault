Distributed Token Economy Explained 分佈式代幣經濟

With the ongoing revolution in information technology, our economic systems of organization are being transformed and disrupted by the rise of information networks. It started with the advent of the personal computer, World Wide Web and with the rise of online platforms the disruptive power of information networks to reshape economic organization became ever more apparent. Today this process of economic transformation continues with a new set of technologies as we are currently in the process of remaking the technology stack of the internet; building what is called web 3.0 a primary component of which is the Blockchain. The defining feature of this next stage of economic development is that it decentralizes our economy and shifts operations to global information based networks like never before. 

隨著資訊技術的不斷革命，資訊網路的崛起正在轉型、破壞我們的經濟組織系統。這始於個人電腦和互聯網的出現，並隨著線上平台的崛起，資訊網路重塑經濟組織的顛覆性力量變得越來越明顯。今日，這個經濟轉型的過程仍在繼續，因為我們目前正在重塑互聯網的技術堆疊——建立所謂的Web 3.0，其主要組成部分就是區塊鏈。這個經濟發展下階段的決定性特徵是，它將使我們的經濟去中心化，並以前所未有的方式將各種營運轉移到基於資訊的全球網路之上。

This distributed internet technology stack that is currently being built enables a network of computers to maintain a collective database of value ownership and exchanges via internet protocols. This bookkeeping is neither closed nor the control of one party, rather, it is public and available in one digital ledger which is distributed across the network. The most mature example of this is what we call the Blockchain. In the Blockchain, all transactions are logged including information on the date, time, participants and amount of every single transaction. On the basis of sophisticated mathematical principles, the transactions are verified by the so-called "miners" who run the computing infrastructure required to maintain the ledgers. 

這個目前正在建立的分佈式互聯網技術堆疊，使電腦網路能夠通過互聯網協議維護一個價值所有權和交易的集體資料庫。這種簿記既不是封閉的，也不是由一方控制的，相反，它是公開的，可以在一個分佈在網路上的數位賬本中獲得。這方面最成熟的例子就是我們所說的區塊鏈。在區塊鏈中，所有的交易都被記錄下來，包括每一筆交易的日期、時間、參與者和金額的資訊。在複雜的數學原理基礎上，交易由所謂的「礦工」——亦即運行維護賬本所需的計算基礎設施的人——來驗證。

The technology of Web 3.0 enables a new form of decentralized economy as it removes the dependency on a centralized authority for managing the network, instead replacing it with a distributed consensus model managed by many. This shared securely encrypted database 

enable trustless peer-to-peer interactions via new internet protocols. People can begin to set up their own networks for coordination and direct exchanges of value peer-to-peer and it enables the rules of these transactions to be automated in new ways. 

  

Web 3.0技術實現了一種新形式的去中心化經濟，因為它消除了對管理網路的中心化權威的依賴，而代之以分佈式共識模式由許多人管理。這種共享的安全加密資料庫通過新的互聯網協議，實現無信任的對等式互動。人們可以開始建立自己的網路，進行協調和對等式直接價值交換，也使這些交易的規則以新的方式自動化。

Tokens 代幣

At the heart of this system is the distributed ledger which records the exchanges of value. These distributed ledgers can account for and validate the exchange of any form of value; it may be a currency, it may be property, it may be a kilowatt-hour of energy, the usage of a parking spot, the number of followers a person has on social media. 

  

這個系統的核心是記錄價值交換的分佈式賬本。這些分佈式賬本可以對任何形式的價值交換進行核算和驗證；它可能是一種貨幣，可能是財產，可能是一千瓦時的能源，一個停車位的使用，也可能是一個人在社交媒體上的粉絲數量。

These distributed ledgers provide the infrastructure for building token economies. A token is simply a quantified unit of value. Tokens are both generic and fungible. It is generic in that it can be used to define any form of value and it is fungible meaning it is exchangeable between different specific forms of value. 

這些分佈式賬本為建立代幣經濟提供了基礎設施。代幣只是一個量化的價值單位。代幣既是通用的，又是可替換的。它是通用的，因為它可以用來定義任何形式的價值；它是可替換的，意味著它可以在不同的特定價值形式之間進行交換。

  

Traditional monetary currencies are not fully fungible as there are many circumstances when one can not exchange a monetary currency for other forms of value. For example, likes on social media may have a certain value but typically can not be directly exchanged for monetary currencies.

  

傳統貨幣不是完全可替代的，因為在很多情況下，人們不能用貨幣兌換其他形式的價值。例如，社交媒體上的喜歡可能有一定的價值，但通常不能直接兌換成實質的金錢貨幣。

  

A token differs from our traditional monetary currency in that it is more generic. Our existing currencies define a particular type of monetary value, what we call utility, which is based upon the economic logic of the industrial economy, while tokens because they are more generic can define a broader set of values, social capital, natural capital or cultural capital. For example, natural capital is the integrity of an ecosystem that enables it to function and provide ecosystems services to people. In our traditional economic model, we only quantify and account for the services that the ecosystem delivers such as food, water, materials etc. However, we do not account for the integrity of the ecosystem that enables it to function. The generic nature of the token means it can be used to account for value such as this natural capital. 

  

代幣與我們傳統的金錢貨幣不同，因為它更加通用。基於工業經濟的經濟邏輯，我們現有的貨幣定義了一種特定類型的貨幣價值，也就是我們所說的效用。而代幣因為更加通用，可以定義更廣泛的價值，包含社會資本、自然資本或文化資本。例如，自然資本是一個生態系統的完整性，使其能夠發揮作用並為人們提供生態系統服務。在傳統經濟模式中，我們只對生態系統提供的服務進行量化和核算，如食物、水、材料等。然而，我們並沒有考慮到使其運作的生態系統的完整性。代幣的通用性意味著它可以用來核算這種自然資本等價值。

The capacity to differentiate between different forms of value is made possible by the programmability of token units. Because tokens are digital they are also programmable, which enables one to specify certain rules for that token and have those rules executed when it is exchanged, thus enabling certain constraints or possibilities in its usage. One can specify that a certain token is only spendable under certain terms or specify how it can be converted. For example, one could program the token so that it can not be exchanged for diamonds that are mined in a particular location of the world known for its use of slave labor. In this way the token is not just a unit of utility but also expresses social values. Likewise one could create a healthcare allowance in dollars or euros could be programmed on the Blockchain so that it can only be used to pay for healthcare at certified parties. Automating these measures leads to a considerable decrease in bureaucracy. This programmable token system works to shift our economies from a single value model to a multi-value model, they create many different types of value and economies but still retain the possibility for exchange between them. 

區分不同形式價值的能力是由代幣單位的可編程性實現的。因為代幣是數位的，它們也是可編程的，這使人們能夠為該代幣指定某些規則，並在它被交換時執行這些規則，從而實現其使用中的某些限制或可能性。人們可以指定某種代幣只能在某些條件下消費，或者指定它如何轉換。例如，如果世界上有某個地方因使用奴隸勞動而聞名，我們可以對代幣進行編程，使其不能被兌換為該地開採的鑽石。這樣一來，代幣就不僅僅是一個效用單位，而且還表達了社會價值。同樣，人們可以在區塊鏈上編程，創建一個美元或歐元的醫療津貼，以便它只能用於支付認證方的醫療費用。這些措施的自動化將使官僚主義大大減少。這種可編程的代幣系統致力於將我們的經濟從單一價值模式轉變為多價值模式，它們創造了許多不同類型的價值和經濟，但仍然保留了它們之間交換的可能性。

Token Markets 代幣市場

The distributed web is the convergence of the economic market system with information technology, that enables us to convert traditional organizations into distributed markets based on tokens. Tokens define whatever is of value within that organization and the market system is used as a distributed coordination mechanism for managing and growing that resource. 

分散式網路是經濟市場體系與資訊技術的融合，它使我們能夠將傳統的組織轉化為基於代幣的分散式市場。代幣定義了該組織內任何有價值的東西，市場系統則被用作管理和增長該資源的分佈式協調機制。

By creating an expanded definition of value and converting closed organizations into open markets this means that we can vastly expand the scope and capacities of the economy. The provisioning of services within the economy no longer becomes dependent upon a limited number of centralized organizations acting for profit, but instead, anyone can now provide the service via these open protocols. This means we can harness the resources of the many in a distributed fashion instead of being dependent upon a few. Likewise, the token economy can harness the motives of individuals not just for financial rewards but for a multiplicity of values. 

通過創建一個擴展的價值定義，並將封閉的組織轉化為開放的市場，這意味著我們可以極大地擴展經濟的範圍和能力。經濟體內服務的提供不再依賴於數量有限的中心化組織的盈利行為，相反，現在任何人都可以通過這些開放協議提供服務。這意味著我們可以以分散式的方式利用許多人的資源，而不是依賴少數人。同樣，代幣經濟可以利用個人的動機，使其不僅僅導向經濟回報，而是為了多元價值。

To illustrate how this works let's think about the service of cloud data storage, currently, this is provided by a limited number of enterprises like Amazon and Microsoft. These centralized organizations have huge data centers, but still, those data centers are only a very small fraction of the storage capacity in the world, most of the storage is in the personal computing devices of end users, and most of that is not being used. Filecoin is one organization that works to create a distributed token economy for this storage. Filecoin is a decentralized storage network that turns cloud storage into an algorithmic market. The market runs on a Blockchain with a native protocol token, also call "Filecoin" which miners earn by providing storage to clients. Conversely, clients spend Filecoins hiring miners to store or distribute data. The sum of all these computers that are coordinated through an automatic market system on the Blockchain can provide, a much larger, more resilient system then the centralized model while reducing redundancy and inefficiencies in the overall system. It also pushes the provision of the service out to the location where it is demanded as people are connecting peer-to-peer locally instead of going to the centralized server that may be the other side of the planet. 

  

為了說明這一點，讓我們想像雲端數據儲存的服務。目前，這是由有限的幾家企業如亞馬遜和微軟提供的。這些中心化組織擁有巨大的數據中心，但這些數據中心仍然只是世界上儲存容量的很小一部分，大部分的儲存是在終端用戶的個人電腦設備中，而其中大部分都沒有被使用。 Filecoin是一個致力於為儲存需求開創分佈式代幣經濟的組織。Filecoin是一個去中心化的儲存網路，將雲端儲存變成一個演算法市場。該市場在區塊鏈上運行，有一個原生協議代幣，也叫「Filecoin」，讓礦工通過向客戶提供儲存空間來賺取。反之，客戶花Filecoins僱傭礦工來儲存或發布數據。所有這些通過區塊鏈上的自動市場系統進行協作的電腦數總和，可以提供一個比中心化模式更大、更有彈性的系統，同時減少整個系統的冗餘和低效率。它還將服務的提供推到有需求的地方，因為人們在本地進行點對點的連接，而不是去找可能在地球另一端的中心化伺服器。

  

Token Investment 代幣投資

Tokens such as Filecoins can be exchanged for other currencies or members can hold on to their tokens whose value may appreciate as the network grows over time. This illustrates a very interesting aspect to tokens; anyone who uses the system is also an investor in the system, thus tokens merge investment capital and liquid exchange capital in new ways. In the traditional capitalist model, we have a divide between owners of capital and workers; a divide between a more fixed investment capital and liquid exchange currencies. The shares in a company are not the same thing as what people get paid with for working in that enterprise and use for everyday exchanges in the market. This creates the notorious divide within the industrial economy described by Karl Marx, between the capitalists - that make money off of their investments - and the workers that have to stay selling their labor for money without ownership. ^[But the dominating/power imbalance structure will still exist, as long as some people are more dependent to others; e.g, members without programming expertise dependent upon the AI experts. -> does this mean that the "ownership of means of production" is not limited to material objects, but also immaterial things such as aptitude? 人們可以將Filecoin等代幣可以兌換成其他貨幣，也可以持續持有這些代幣，因其價值可能隨著網路的發展而升值。這說明了代幣的一個非常有趣的面向——任何使用該系統的人都是該系統的投資者，因此代幣以新的方式融合了投資資本和流動資本。在傳統的資本主義模式中，我們有個資本所有者和工人之間的鴻溝，以及比較固定的投資資本和流動的交易貨幣之間的鴻溝。公司股份與人們在該企業工作所獲得的報酬，和在市場上用於日常交流的東西不是一回事。這就造成了馬克思所描述的工業經濟中臭名昭著的鴻溝，即資本家從投資中賺錢，而工人因沒有所有權不得不一直為錢出賣自己的勞動。

Tokens represent both the inherent value of the community, which is its capital investment, and they are also units of exchange within that ecosystem. The founders of the project issue a number of tokens at inception and sell those, for someone to use the system they have to buy the tokens, in so doing they become part investors in the project but they also use those same tokens to make exchanges within the market. Thus the people creating the value in the ecosystem are also getting paid in tokens, meaning the workers that are creating the value through their work also have ownership within the organization. 

代幣既代表了社群的固有價值，也就是它的資本投資，同時它們也是該生態系統中的交易單位。專案創始人在成立之初就發行了一些代幣，並出售這些代幣，如果有人要使用這個系統，他們必須購買這些代幣，這樣他們就成為專案的部分投資者，但他們也用這些代幣在市場上進行交易。因此，在生態系統中創造價值的人也得到了代幣的報酬，這意味著通過工作創造價值的工人在組織內也有所有權。

In the traditional utility based exchange of cash people have no ownership in the organization, they just try to make money and this can create divides between the owners and the users. The token system works to better align the incentives of the individuals with the overall system because the value of the tokens they earn is also dependent upon the value of the whole. When you are working for a token network you are both working for yourself and for the whole organization as the two become more aligned, unlike the traditional divide between capitalist and worker.

在傳統的基於效用的現金交換中，人們在組織中沒有所有權，他們只是試圖賺錢，這可能會在所有者和用戶之間產生分歧。代幣系統試圖將個人的獎勵與整個系統更好地結合起來，因為他們賺取的代幣的價值也取決於整個系統的價值。當你為代幣網路付出時，你既是在為自己工作，也是在為整個組織工作，因為兩者變得更加一致，而不像傳統的資本家和工人之間的分界。

  

Economic Growth 經濟成長

The token system enables networks to overcome the chicken and egg problem. If you are the first user of a network like eBay, then the value would be very low, thus it is difficult to get the network started because it has to reach a critical mass before it will be of value to the users; this means that it may require a large investment to create a network. The Silicon Valley model worked by having large initial venture capital backing that enables them to overcome this, but it means that most networks don't get off the ground and that once a network reaches scale and has value it becomes dominant and very difficult to compete with; resulting in a lock-in effect and making it easy for large incumbent organizations to become extractive over time. It also means that those who founded the organization win big time. If the network takes off, it creates a winner takes all dynamic with most people losing because of the threshold. 

代幣系統使網路能夠克服「雞生蛋、蛋生雞」的問題。如果你是像eBay這樣的網路的第一個用戶，那麼價值會非常低，因此很難讓網路開始運作，因為它必須達到一個臨界用戶量才會對用戶有價值；這意味著可能需要大量的投資來創建一個網路。矽谷模式的成功在於有大量的初始風險資本支持，使他們能夠克服這個問題。但這意味著大多數網路不能起步，因為一旦一個網路達到規模並具有價值，它就會成為主導者，使其他網路很難與之競爭；這導致鎖定效應，使大型在位組織容易隨著時間推移而成為榨取者。這也意味著創立該組織的人大獲全勝。在這種模式下，一旦某個網路成功了，也就創造了一個贏家通吃的動態，使其他多數人因為門檻過高而損失。

The token system extends the benefits of being an early adopter of a new network to all the users and thus helps to solve this issue. It does this by issuing tokens for anyone to purchase at the beginning of the project, as the project grows the tokens come to have greater value for all of the holders. 

  

代幣系統將作為新網路早期使用者的利益擴展到所有用戶，從而有助於解決這個問題。它通過在專案開始時發行代幣供任何人購買，讓代幣隨著專案的發展，對所有持有者帶來更大的價值。

  

This also works to make the users of the network promoters of that organization because as it grows the tokens that they hold become more valuable. It incentivizes people to join networks early so as to gain the benefits of the increase in their token value as it grows which reduces the problem of thresholds. With this technology companies no longer have to go to traditional capital markets through an initial public offering of shares in the company in exchange for money, but instead they simply sell tokens directly on the internet to raise initial capital for the project in what is called an initial coin offering or ICO. 

  

這也使網路的用戶成為該組織的推廣者，因為隨著網路的發展，他們所持有的代幣將變得更有價值。它激勵人們儘早加入網路，以便隨著網路的發展，獲得代幣價值增加的好處，這就減少了門檻問題。有了這種技術，公司不再需要通過首次公開發行公司股份來換取資金，而是直接在互聯網上出售代幣，為專案籌集初始資金，這就是所謂的首次代幣發行或ICO。

  

Conclusion 結論

To date we have had an internet patched onto the side of an economy operated through the many centralized organizations of the Industrial Age, creating a strong contradiction between the underlying technology and the institutional arrangements. The Distributed Web will work to transform this by merging information networks and economic organization as the flow of information and economic value becomes one. This will greatly reduce our dependence on centralized organizations expanding markets as systems of organization. The global market economy will become available to the many through small distributed peer-to-peer interactions running through web protocols as the decentralized internet takes us a step further into the networked economy.

  

迄今為止，我們已經有了一個互聯網，黏著於以中心化組織為基底運作的工業經濟，在基礎技術和制度安排之間產生了強烈的矛盾。分佈式網路將通過合併資訊網路和經濟組織來改變這種狀況，因為資訊流和經濟價值成為一體。這將大大減少我們對中心化組織的依賴，擴大市場作為組織系統。隨著去中心化的互聯網使我們向網路經濟又邁進了一步，全球市場經濟將通過通過網路協議運行的小型分佈式點對點互動而為許多人所用。
