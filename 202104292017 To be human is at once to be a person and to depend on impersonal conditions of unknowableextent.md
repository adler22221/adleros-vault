To be human is at once to be a person and to depend on impersonal conditions of unknowable
extent. A human economy rests on bringing together two ideas – restoring to people greater control
over their everyday affairs; and acknowledging that everyone participates in the human predicament
as a whole.^[another point I resonate to mention in [[202104221106 Keith Hart misunderstanding]]] How then to develop meaningful and practical links between the two?
