# Mission

> Drafted ≈a year ago, let's discuss what should be updated; in particular, the strategies--especially the 2nd one--is partially at fault for my physical burnout. New strategies need to be developed that keeps the ecosystem hub spirit without overwhelming my health.

**What I repeatedly do to realize the Vision** 

- Investigate 1) the common underlying mechanisms and 2) their enabling/sustaining conditions that lead to the emergence of planetary harms or civilizational crises as wicked problems.

- Explore agent-based and cooperative approaches to transforming wicked problems by “being the change.”

- Ideal characteristics of such agent-based and cooperative approaches include, but are not limited to 

	a) having the power to redirect the dynamics of a system (patterns of thinking, acting, interacting) and its trajectory of emergence, while having 

	b) low money dependence (change can happen without possessing much financial resource), 

	c) low medium dependence (change can happen by utilizing whatever medium is at their disposal), and 

	d) not generally requiring unreachable entry barriers (change can happen without helplessly waiting for policy change or power over resource-intensive technologies).

**My major strategies**
- Conduct social research that informs change model design and prototyping. During both processes, participants undergo self- and communal transformation as education.

- Hold positions simultaneously at different organizations or projects to serve as a connective and prototyping hub that facilitates synergies across the ecosystem for systems transformation.
