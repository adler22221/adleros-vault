# Vision

> Based on drafts ≈a year ago, let's discuss what should be updated. 

To leave the world with seeds for mindful, regenerative, and convivial ecologies. 

By seeds I mean the explicit and implicit knowledge/wisdom as foundation, plus agents (and better, communities of agents) that dissolve the mechanisms that perpetuate planetary harm by transforming themselves—through agent-based, low-barrier, mindful ways of becoming that regenerate our social, ecological, and inner worlds. 

The knowledge/wisdom maps to my embodied research-knowledge production, grounded in praxis/lived-experiences/observations-empathy/etc; the communities maps to my attempts to form ecosystem hubs, make awakening, empowering and sustained alliances/networks among people; and agents maps to my attempts to awaken and empower people, facilitating mutual/planetary care as/through collective self-actualization/trancendance (cf. 黃俊傑：身心一如、成己成人不二、天人合一). These three strands together, make what I call "research and praxis as education."

At the heart of this is this shared responsibility — a kind of collective karma — and the understanding that genuine change begins not with waiting, blaming, or escaping, but with embodied transformation or be(com)ing the change. We can take care of the world by enabling each other’s self-actualization/trancendance.
