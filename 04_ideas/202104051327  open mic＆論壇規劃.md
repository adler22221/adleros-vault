\### Structure of a session: <= 希望互動性高，減少由上而下之感。

\- Story telling

\- Breakout groups

\- For the audience to interact with the open mic challengers

\- Form the basis of observation for evaluation

\- Public grading

\- Speaker's comment and live story sharing

-   每個storyteller 是一個 group，大家可以輪流進入不同的 group。每個參與者都是一個評價者，透過分組互動去給這些人評價；最後要有一個螢幕顯示整體反饋狀況。
    
-   最後由兩位講者兼評審，總評並分享自己故事。
    

  
  

Set rubrics for the audiences to grade as well
