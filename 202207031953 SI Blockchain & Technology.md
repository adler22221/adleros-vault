Blockchain & Technology 區塊鏈與科技

A new technology paradigm is emerging as we move into this world of connectivity it is called the Internet of Things, IoT for short. 

當我們進入這個充滿連結的世界時，一種新的技術典範正在出現，它被稱為物聯網，簡稱IoT。

The technology paradigm of the industrial age was one of machines, stand-alone mechanical systems, they were physical in nature, mono-functional and mechanized; it is a world of nonresponsive, isolated technologies each of which the end user has to manage separately. 

工業時代的技術典範是機器、獨立的機械系統，它們具有物理性質，功能單一且機械化；這是個沒有反應的、孤立的技術世界，必須由每個終端用戶各自管理。

The technology paradigm of the Information Age is one of services, it is a world where individual component technologies are instrumented and connected into larger networks of devices that can communicate peer-to-peer, adapt and self-organize around the users needs so as to deliver a seamless service. The best illustration of this is the smart city, where different systems no longer exist in silos but are interconnected and organized around the end users needs. 

資訊時代的技術典範是種服務典範，在這個世界，單個組件技術被儀器化並連接到更大的設備網路中；這些設備可以對等式地交流，適應並圍繞用戶的需求進行自我組織，從而提供無縫服務。這方面最好的說明是智慧城市，在那裡不同的系統不再是孤立存在的，而是圍繞著終端用戶的需求相互連接和組織。

The Internet of Things is a journey we are just beginning on over the course of the next decade billions of devices will come online, the amount of data the internet has to deal with will grow massively as vast networks of machines continuously communicate with each other, to coordinate production processes, for transport and logistics, for construction, climate control etc. 

物聯網是一個我們剛剛展開的旅程，在未來十年中，數十億的設備將上線，互聯網必須處理的數據量將大量增加，因為巨大的機器網路不斷地相互溝通，以協調生產過程，用於運輸和物流，用於建築，氣候控制等等。

This will require an IT infrastructure that goes far beyond the existing capacities of the internet in terms of dealing with massive amounts of secure data, secure communication transactions and automated micro exchanges of value between systems and devices on the edges of networks. This is part of what the next evolution in the internet will have to deal with, a world of upwards of 20 billion devices constantly streaming data and making secure micro exchanges between each other. Put simply this world of a fully fledged IoT infrastructure to our economies would be an almost unimaginably complex system. The issues here can be thought of in threefold, firstly the bottlenecks created by existing cloud-based IoT systems secondly issues surrounding the security risks of centralized systems, and thirdly enabling automated dynamic resource allocation between machines. 

這將需要一種處理大量的安全數據、安全通信交易、網路邊緣系統和設備之間的自動微觀價值交換方面，都遠遠超出互聯網現有能力的新IT基礎設施。這是互聯網下一步發展必須面對的局面：一個由超過200億台設備組成的世界，不斷地流轉數據並在彼此之間進行安全的微觀交換。簡單地說，這個成熟的物聯網基礎設施的世界對我們的經濟來說將是一個幾乎無法想像的複雜系統。這裡的問題可以從三個方面來考慮：首先是現有的基於雲端的物聯網系統造成的瓶頸，其次是圍繞中心化系統的安全風險問題，第三是實現機器之間的自動動態資源分配。

Centralized Systems 中心化系統

At present IoT systems often depend upon a centralized architecture where information is sent from the device to a proprietary cloud where the data is processed using analytics and then sent back to those tiny IoT devices to coordinate them. As with all centralized systems, this doesn't scale well. In a world of complexity, you have to have much more processing and coordination out on the edges, with coordination happening peer to peer so as to reduce the bottlenecks and centralized security vulnerabilities. In order to scale you need to be able to make the decisions locally, process the data locally and also be able to share resources locally between devices on demand. Likewise, security is, of course, a huge issue with the

internet of things, more and more we are connecting our critical infrastructure up to the IoT a good example being the development of the smart grid or urban transport coordination systems. The blockchain is promising for IoT security for the same reasons it works for cryptocurrency: It provides assurances that data is legitimate, and the process that introduces new data is well-defined. 

  

目前，物聯網系統通常依賴於一個中心化架構，資訊從設備發送到專有的雲端，在那裡使用分析技術處理數據，然後發回給那些微小的物聯網設備以進行協調。與所有中心化系統一樣，這並不能很好地擴展。在一個複雜的世界裡，你必須在邊緣有更多的處理和協調，協調是對等式的，以便減少瓶頸和集中的安全漏洞。為了擴大規模，你需要能夠在本地做出決定，在本地處理數據，並且能夠在本地設備之間按需共享資源。同樣，當我們連接了越來越多關鍵基礎設施到物聯網，安全也成了一個巨大問題。智能電網或城市交通協調系統的發展就是個好例子。區塊鏈有望提升物聯網的安全，原因與它對加密貨幣的作用一樣。它提供了數據合法的保證，而且引入新數據的過程是明確的。

A decentralized approach to IoT networking could solve many issues. Adopting a standardized peer-to-peer communication model to process the hundreds of billions of transactions between devices will significantly reduce the costs associated with installing and maintaining large centralized data centers and will distribute computation and storage needs across the billions of devices that form IoT networks. This will prevent failure in any single node in a network from shutting down the whole network. 

物聯網網路的分散式方法可以解決許多問題。採用標準化的對等式通信模型來處理設備之間數以千億計的交易，將大大減少與安裝和維護大型中心數據中心有關的成本，並將計算和儲存需求分配到構成物聯網網路的數十億設備上。這將防止網路中任何單一節點故障導致整個網路的關閉。

By leveraging the blockchain, IoT solutions can enable secure, trustless messaging between devices in an IoT network. In this model, the blockchain will treat message exchanges between devices similar to financial transactions in a bitcoin network. 

通過利用區塊鏈，物聯網解決方案可以在物聯網網路的設備之間實現安全、無信任的資訊傳遞。在這種模式下，區塊鏈將把設備之間的資訊交流與比特幣網路中的金融交易類似。

Resource Allocation 資源配置

The other major issue is that of dynamic resource allocation. Just as a money economy allows for better, faster, and more efficient allocation of resources on a human scale, a machine economy can provide a robust and decentralized system for handling these same issues on a machine scale. Thus this internet of things is going to need a micro payment system where devices can pay on demand per resource, this won't work with transaction fees or having to wait for centralized third parties for authentication. 

  

另一個主要問題是動態資源分配問題。正如貨幣經濟允許在人類範圍內更好、更快、更有效地分配資源一樣，機器經濟可以提供一個強大的、去中心化的系統來處理機器範圍內的這些相同問題。因此，這個物聯網將需要一個微型支付系統，設備可以按需求支付每項資源，而這不是交易費用或等待中心化第三方來認證所能達到的。

We really want to make it possible for one machine that has excess capacity or excess resources, like for example computational power storage or electricity, to be able to sell that to another machine which needs it more. One example often given of this is autonomous vehicle automatically negotiating highway passage if they are in a hurry, paying other cars to let them pass first.^[Highly plausible unintended consequence: People are incentivized to get ahead of & block others to get paid for bypassing -> still falls prey to [[Money Dependence 貨幣依存]]] Coordinating air delivery drones is another potential use case for device-to-device micropayment where drones may pay a solar recharging station belonging to someone else to get charged up with electricity. 

我們真的希望讓一台擁有過剩能力或過剩資源的機器，例如運算能力、儲存空間或電力，能夠將其出售給另一台更需要它的機器。這方面經常舉出的一個例子是，自動駕駛車輛在趕時間的情況下自動協商高速公路的通行，付錢給其他車輛，讓他們先通過。協調空中送貨的無人機是設備對設備小額支付的另一個潛在用案例，無人機可以向屬於其他人的太陽能充電站付費，以獲得電力。

Plug and Play 即插即用

The internet of things will be all about networks that deliver services, where any device that can contribute to the overall function of the network will be able to automatically plug into the network offing its capacities and receiving tokens in exchange. This kind of computing environment needs a kind of permissionless innovation layer where anyone can start participating and contributing to any network. Whether that is providing a 3D printer within a manufacturing network, a bicycle in a transport network or a room in an accommodation network. A connected device will be able to provide services and receiving payments for work done that are automatically credited to the owner's wallet. This will create all sorts of security, privacy and financial issues that the blockchain is well suited to enable as data can reside with the end device and made available only when and as needed.

  

物聯網將是關於提供服務的網路，任何能夠為網路的整體功能做出貢獻的設備將能夠自動插入網路，提供其能力並接受代幣作為交換。這種計算環境需要一種無許可的創新層，使任何人都可以開始參與和貢獻於任何網路。無論是在製造網路中提供一台3D影印機，在運輸網路中提供一輛自行車，還是在住宿網路中提供一個房間。一個連接的設備將能夠提供服務，並為所做的工作接收付款，這些付款將自動記入所有者的錢包。這將產生各種安全、隱私和財務問題，而區塊鏈很適合解決這些問題，因為數據可以存放在終端設備中，只有在需要的時候才能使用。

  

One example of this is the DAV network, which stands for decentralized autonomous vehicles. 

  

這方面的一個例子簡稱「DAV」的分散式自動駕駛車輛網路。

  

DAV is a blockchain platform currently under development that allows anyone to buy or sell autonomous transportation services in a decentralized market. Their platform integrates into any autonomous vehicles, enabling those vehicles to discover, communicate, and transact with one another using a digital token called DAV. People can earn DAV Tokens by providing transportation services, a self-driving car could give rides to passengers when not needed by its owner or a drone could help with a delivery 

  

DAV是一個目前正在開發的區塊鏈平台，允許任何人在一個分散式的市場上購買或出售自動駕駛運輸服務。任何自動駕駛車輛都可以整合到這個平台，使這些車輛能夠使用一個名為DAV的數位代幣相互發現、溝通和交易。人們可以通過提供運輸服務來賺取DAV代幣，一輛自動駕駛汽車可以在車主不需要的時候讓乘客搭車，或者無人機可以幫助送貨。

  

IOTA 

Blockchain technology is the missing link to settle scalability, privacy, and reliability concerns in the Internet of Things but of course the blockchain infrastructure of the present could never scale to the demand that is needed, new blockchain infrastructure will have to evolve. Probably the most promising project in this respect is IOTA which aims to be the distributed network protocol enabling the machine economy. 

  

區塊鏈技術是解決物聯網中可擴展性、隱私和可靠性問題的缺失環節，但當然，目前的區塊鏈基礎設施沒有能力擴展到所需的需求，因此新的區塊鏈基礎設施將不得不發展起來。在這方面最有希望的項目可能是IOTA，它的目標是成為實現機器經濟的分散式網路協議。

The main innovation behind IOTA is what they call the Tangle, a new distributed ledger design which is scalable, lightweight and makes it possible to transfer value without any fees. The Tangle ledger is able to settle transactions with zero fees so devices can trade exact amounts of resources on-demand, as well as store data from sensors and data loggers securely and verified on the ledger. 

IOTA背後的主要創新是他們所謂的Tangle，一個新的分散式賬本設計，它是可擴展的，輕量級的，並使價值轉移成為可能，沒有任何費用。Tangle賬本能夠以零費用進行交易結算，因此設備可以按需交易精確數量的資源，以及安全地儲存來自傳感器和數據記錄器的數據並在賬本上進行驗證。

While the whole world has adopted the ‘Sharing Economy’ in areas like driving and accommodation, IOTA enables a whole new realm where anything with a chip in it can be leased in real time. Most of our belongings stay idle for the vast majority of the time that we possess ownership of them, but through blockchain IoT networks a lot of these things like Appliances, Tools, Drones, eBikes etc. and resources such as computer storage, computational power, WiFi bandwidth etc. can be turned into leasing-services effortlessly.

當全世界都在駕駛和住宿等領域採用「共享經濟」時，IOTA實現了一個全新的領域，使任何帶有芯片的東西都可以實時租賃。在我們擁有所有權的絕大部分時間裡，我們的大部分物品都是閒置的，但通過區塊鏈物聯網網路，很多這些東西，如家電、工具、無人機、電動自行車等，以及計算機存儲、計算能力、WiFi帶寬等資源都可以毫不費力地變成租賃服務。
