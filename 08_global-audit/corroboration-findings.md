### (a) Systemic / bottom-up / cooperative values — predate the "recent window"
**Actual (documented), confirmed to 2015, well before 2018:**
The "青醒人七大核心價值" doc (created 2015-02-25) states, near-verbatim:
- 面對真理，永遠謙卑 (humility before truth)
- 反求諸己 (self-examination first, not blame-externalizing)
- 做彼此心靈的靠山 (mutual/peer support)
- 平等無階級 (equality, no hierarchy)
- "From competition to contribution"
- "From outside-in to inside-out"
- "From fighting/standing against to fighting/standing for"

The "願景使命宣言" (created 2015-03-16) frames the mission as "自我引導" (self-directed guidance), "系統探究" (systemic inquiry), and "理想社會搖籃" (cradle for an ideal society) — explicitly systemic, self-directed, and anti-hierarchical/anti-scapegoating language, two-plus years before the 2018 threshold and 9+ years before the "recent window" (2024-06 onward) the audit was originally scoped to.

This directly corroborates clm-0006's claim that the core value set (systemic, bottom-up, cooperative, democratic, anti-scapegoating/anti-top-down/anti-confrontational/anti-individualist-heroism) is independently verifiable in GDrive-era documents and stable well before 2009... **with one caveat**: the earliest artifact found in this pass is dated 2015, not 2009. The 2009 anchor (documentary filmmaking origin) is corroborated as a *motivational* origin point (see below) but the *values statement itself* is only documented from 2015 onward in the files inspected. This is a minor confidence adjustment, not a refutation — it still predates the 2024-2026 "re-marking" window by 9+ years.

### (b) Dependence/exhaustion theory family — predates 2018 requested threshold, found even earlier
**Actual (documented):**
- "The Linear Process of Exhaustion.docx" (undated-prefix, created 2017-10-12, modified 2017-11-06) is the earliest full draft found — this predates even the 2018 threshold specified in the task.
- The 2018-04-21 draft ("Working Draft") already contains the mature theory: ranking/tracking systems as a "funneling" mechanism producing economic, socio-political, health, and environmental crises via a "Linear Process of Exhaustion" — dependence on resource/functional-value allocation, mutual exploitation in market economies, biochemical/craving-based consumption cycles, and environmental exhaustion via linear (non-cyclical) resource use. This is a fully-formed dependence-theory precursor, textually close to what later becomes his "capacital/dependence" framework.
- "In Search of Modes of Coexistence" (2018-05-13 draft, also 2018-05-04 draft) documents the systemic/relational-causation framing (all suffering derives from "modes of coexistence") and explicitly self-dates the underlying motivation to "age 14" and "childhood," with the documentary work as the operational starting point.

**Distinguishing "actual" from "real" (per Adler's own framing):** The documents found are exactly the kind of "actual" (documented) layer he pointed to — they establish that the theoretical architecture (exhaustion, dependence, systemic coexistence) existed in written form by 2017-2018, well before the 2024-2026 window. They do **not**, on their own, establish the "real" (retroductive/autoethnographic) layer — i.e., that personal lived-dependence experience *generated* the theory rather than the theory being professionally/academically motivated first. The 2018 draft's "Motivation and Intent" section does personalize the drive ("this deep concern... has always been the main drive... since childhood"), which lends some support to the personal-genesis reading, but this is Adler's own narrative framing within the document, not independent triangulation. The bodhisattva-path / "我不入地獄誰入地獄" framing specifically (clm-0051's most distinctive claim) was **not found verbatim** in any of the documents inspected — the closest analogue is the generic "compassionate instinct"/suffering-witnessing language in the 2018 Modes of Coexistence draft. The specific Buddhist/Christian role-model framing appears to be a member-check-stage articulation, not yet corroborated in the archival record.

**Net assessment:** clm-0051 and clm-0006's core claims about pre-2018/pre-2009 stability of values and dependence-theory are CORROBORATED at the "actual" level for 2015 (values) and 2017-2018 (theory). The specific bodhisattva/autoethnographic-generative-motivation claim remains PARTIALLY corroborated — plausible and consistent with tone, but not independently documented pre-2018 in the files reviewed.
