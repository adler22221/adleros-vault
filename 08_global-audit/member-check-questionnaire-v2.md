# Member-Check Questionnaire v2 — Phase 3 instrument

**For**: Adler Yang. **Covers**: all 38 C3 claims + all 7 implicit/real-stratum candidates (⚑) from `claims.jsonl` v2 (45 rows).

**How to respond.** Mark one option per row; add a line for any Nuance.

- **Confirm** — the statement matches your lived reality as worded. Confidence may rise at the next re-rating.
- **Deny** — the claim is **pulled** from the persona. Your denial is recorded as data alongside the trace evidence (per protocol we do not argue it; a denial is itself a finding about the gap between record and self-account).
- **Nuance(____)** — you reword the statement; the reworded version replaces the original and its confidence is **capped at C3** (implicit candidates stay C2) until re-evidenced.

⚑ rows are retroduced *candidate mechanisms*, not observations — for these, "Confirm" means "this reading rings truer than the rival listed," not "this is proven." Evidence pointers are tier-safe segment ids; no sensitive verbatim is reproduced here.
