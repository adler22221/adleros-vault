# [[Lai, Karyn]]: Encountering the world: Zhuangzi on responsive agency

Mastery stories, how they have learned their skills
cultivation, learning

## political statement

animals act irrationality(?)
the stories sound like children fables
these stories are written as responses
to officials preoccupied with Confucianism 
--> tell them animal stories! former criminals, people with wierd appearances... spokespersons with no respect of the day

seemingly disrespectful naked painter
not praising the product, because there's nothing yet!

Zhuangzi's text challenge our expectations
highlight contrasted ways of life --> he see one promoted way of life problematic (although Zhuangzi's portrait of such conformism also narrow)
promoters of that kind of life are also in chains!
shake people out of being confortable of what they're doing

## what do the mastery stories offer?

4 features:
- excellence in performing
	- with product: e.g., ziqin(?)
	- without product: e.g, swiming
- engaging with relevant conditions, i.e., no one is applying preconceived notions of what to do now
	- e.g., Pao Ding
- use capacities for the task
- cultivates capacities

who are the speakers/spokesperson in the stories?
痀僂者、津人's spokesperson is Confucius

## cultivation: the heavenly and the human

seem to be hostile against human endeavors
its about tien and ren aligning
ren 人 cannot mie tien 滅天

tien isn't supernatural, but natural conditions
fit and alignment with tien

ziqin, there's an ideal fit
"matching heaven with heaven"

impact of forgetting is facinating [^1]

forget something to be attend to something
if you look at so much you lost the mastery

conceptions of knowing-that, knowing-how are present

is there higher order control?
trying not to try --> self-defeating
if you have higher order control, you can't forget anymore
you have to plan before you can't get it right, but at the point of execution, you can't think about it anymore
occurant(?)

at the point of execution, they don't exert control
