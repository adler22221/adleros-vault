# Inspired by [[Mulgan, Geoff]]’s [[Another World is Possible]]

[[德中學 Learning by Caring|learning by caring]]
Allows one to learn generatively
Which is, to not take what currently is for granted
Not forcing oneself to fit into the measures of current knowledge system

Because the purpose is to care
The inherently partial temporal fallible knowledge is just a raft [[筏喻]] for us to reach the other end 彼岸

The existing paradigm of knowledge and learning reproduces [[Allocation Dependence 配分依存|allocation dependence]]
In which the size of the totality of existing knowledge forms the boundary of what can be learned 
(Cf [[Mulgan, Geoff]]’s idea of [[Intellectual prisons]] )

It is assumed that only by mastering what’s in this boundary that one can go beyond this boundary 
Or by filling the mold of the knowledge-brick that one could eventually build another brick on the existing ones

Learning by caring allows one to sense /be inspired by relevant information
Without being propelled to internalize what is irrelevant or inapplicable
