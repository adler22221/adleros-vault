# Thien Quach
Thien Quach is an entrepreneur, (un)educator, financial advisor, social experiment conductor, and experiencer who senses what emerges for a more beautiful world. He is the founder and co-founder of: - The Soil Project - an international alternative higher education and gap year program - VCIL Community - a network and community of people who advocate paradigm shift, sustainable/regenerative development through alternative (non-traditional) education, and entrepreneurship. - Starfish Community to advocate entrepreneurship, promote financial literacy, and raise emotional intelligence with money for young people. -- His effort is to learn and create enterprises in reimagining education, tourism, economy, and business for a regenerative future.


# Bechem Ayuk
BECHEM F. AYUK is a dynamic and confident Christian, education enthusiast, public speaker and and social leader. One of his main missions in life is to revive the educational system by making it more practical, ethical, creative and accessible to all. He is the founder of Brilliant Redemption; a youth-led organisation created to redeem humanity through quality education.

His passion for education propelled him to start the BLACK EDUCATION MASTERCLASS; an online school in which we taught the great things about AFRICA which are not being taught in schools because our educational system is corrupt and messed up. Bechem strongly believes that one of AFRICA’s biggest problems is ignorance so he organized the BLACK EDUCATION MASTERCLASS to decolonize African minds for African unity and prosperity. This masterclass has decolonized the minds of over 300Africans in just the first 2 editions so far.

His commitment to providing better education to all made him volunteer to be one of the facilitators at the Goodwill Fellowship Program, a youth program aimed at creating a new breed of ethical problem-solvers. He has also spoken at several events such the Youths Against Hatespeech workshop organised by We Win Team, the International African Youth Conference organised by O.Y.P.A and the Natasha Mwansa Foundation discussion panel.

His passion for redeeming AFRICA also caught the attention of the AFRICAN ANTIDOTE MOVEMENT and they made him their representative in the beautiful country of Cameroon.

Bechem Ayuk is a Bachelor's Degree holder in Sociology from the University of Yaounde 1, and equally has 2 certificates(Project Management Leadership and Introduction to Coaching and Mentoring) issued to him by the Humanitarian Leadership Academy. He is also a graduate from the transformative Basil Henry Mentorship Institute and currently serves as the president of the BAHMI Graduates' Centre.

His work caught the eye of ELS EDIFICATION PLUS as they awarded him with the International Young Humanitarian Award in 2020.

He is equally passionate about leadership, reading, youth empowerment, learning, educating, diplomacy, the Beautiful Game(Football) and always spreads the message of unity(his first name BECHEM actually means 'to unite') oneness and living an UBUNTU life; I am because we are. How can one be happy when the others are sad?
