Adler:
Can existing systems methods (e.g., SD, soft systems, design thinking, etc.) complement ABA or vice versa?

Derek Cabrera 11:02 PM 
Good question. SD, soft systems, design thinking, etc are manifestations of DSRP structure that highlight certain thing and lowlight others. In that sense, they introduce bias which is sometimes acceptable if it is by design. But perhaps better is to understand the system and build the model on the fly using DSRP. In this way, you can not be limited by the model bias and can be more fluid…
