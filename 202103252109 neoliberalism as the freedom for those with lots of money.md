We have just been through a prolonged social experiment in which
markets and money were left to fnd their own way around the
world without much political interference. This experiment has been
called ‘[[neoliberalism]]’, at one time ‘the Washington consensus’. ==The
freedom of these markets was mainly for those with lots of money.==^[A brilliant way of describing [[neoliberalism]]]
The rest of us had to adjust; and society became much more unequal
as a result. One victim of [[neoliberalism]] was certainly democracy in
any meaningful sense.
