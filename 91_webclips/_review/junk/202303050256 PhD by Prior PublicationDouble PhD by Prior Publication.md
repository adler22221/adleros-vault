### PhD by Prior Publication or Existing Published

  

           Doctor of Philosophy by Prior Publication or Existing Published or Series of Publication is a doctoral-level award and it is being awarded to the persons who have published books or articles in journals or articles in magazines or in similar means. Contents of the publications must be new knowledge contributions and contemporary. Scientific contents can be backdated up to 10 years and it is still acceptable for consideration. In the [**Medical**](https://www.advan-kt.com/doctorofmedicine) field, 3 ISI journal publications are required while in Engineering or Science field, 5 journal publications are primarily counted for. Any publication in the conference series will completely be rejected unless it has ISI-series status. The contents must have coherence in their order for journal publication and as for the book publication, it is not so. The normal procedure is that the eligible candidates have to apply through our system, which is given [**here**](https://forms.gle/HaP46TKYgHHPryVRA) . The required documents are i) a list of publications, ii) a detailed CV, and iii) a photograph. Three members of the panel will evaluate the application once it reaches us, as to finalize and recommend it to the senate of Universities. However, the final decision lies in the hand of the University's senate. The fee is a must in this mode after the successful acceptance by universities. A minimum of 3 months and a maximum of 12 months may consume for the entire process or so. The Ph.D. Viva Voce Exam is compulsory for this mode but skype presentation is acceptable for exceptional cases. The [**double Ph.D. by Prior Publications**](https://www.advan-kt.com/doublephdbypub) is also possible in future.
