## Product description

![](https://www.olaf-scooter.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/urban_riding-845x684.jpg)

## Urban’s riding experience.

The best riding experience is based on a wooden platform and cleverly designed steering system with a special high carbon front axis that absorbs road bumps. The advanced steering system allows sharp turns while still handling great speeds.

## The patented folding mechanism.

The mechanism was adapted from the OLAF Business model, which allows you to easily switch from trolley to kick-scooter with just your foot, without any bending. It also has an additional lever for better support when OLAF is in a vertical position or used as a trolley.

![](https://www.olaf-scooter.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/urban_wheels_NEW-845x684.jpg)

## The ergonomically designed backpack.

It is fully incorporated within the URBAN chassis, and it can be removed instantly. Hidden wheel cover pockets are added to keep your back clean when worn as backpack. It is put together with durable stitching using the best waterproof materials and additionally upgraded with reective material patches (for night visibility).

## Open mobility platform

The URBAN mobility platform is all about the open community approach. Due to the unique and sturdy platform, OLAF team was able to go a step further and incorporate a system that allows you to attach your own backpack or similar luggage (grocery bag, camera bag for professional photographers, larger women’s bags, etc.)
