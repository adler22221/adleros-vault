# Step 2 Audit — 2026-05-15

## §A — Introduction-5 typology of five predicaments (Step 2A)

The 2026-04-29 Introduction-5 draft lists five predicaments at five registers:
1. **Adaptive** (Evolutionary Register) — biological adaptation incommensurability
2. **Epistemic** (Cognitive Register) — Stiegler grammatization expropriating cognitive capacity
3. **Governance** (Political Register) — algorithmic governance outpacing democratic deliberation
4. **Livelihood** (Economic Register) — Illich radical monopoly capturing livelihood
5. **Existential** (Ontological Register) — metaverse self-completing tendency transforming what it means to be human

### Verdict: Introduction-5 holds substantively; three minor adjustments needed before integration into Intro_v1

1. **"Existential" → "anthropological-ontological"** (or similar). The current label is phenomenology-laden and risks the register Mi pushed back against. The substantive content (metaverse-stage transforming what human existence is) is anthropological-ontological. Adjusts to the squeeze-ontology lock's vocabulary (Level 2 worlds-as-systems-of-means; §IV anthropological stakes).
2. **"Technology" references** in Introduction-5's text should be relabelled to "artificiality" per the post-2026-05-14 §IIA disclaimer (technology dropped as major thesis concept; Stiegler-Obara triangulation supports artificiality as umbrella).
3. **"Self-completing tendency"** terminology should be confirmed consistent with lock §1.3 + S1-11_v4 §E (autopoietic outcome of one mode of artificiality, not external).

### Key finding: Introduction-5 registers map cleanly onto the body's five pre-squeeze diagnosticians

The 2B audit found that **the body explicitly names five thinkers** as pre-metaverse-squeeze diagnosticians: Stiegler / Obara / Frankfurt / Illich / Hui. These map cleanly onto the Introduction-5 five registers:

| Introduction-5 Register | Body diagnostician | Body location |
|---|---|---|
| Adaptive (biological) | Obara (self-petification via capsulization) | Ch-6-3 v1 §2; Ch-5-5 §2.1; squeeze-ontology lock §3 |
| Epistemic (cognitive) | Stiegler (grammatization → proletarianization) | Ch-6-3 v1 §3; Ch-5-5 §1, §2; Ch-2-5 |

The conclusion can cite either or both, with the body as source of truth.

## §C — Consequential decisions surfaced by the audit

### Decision 1 — §IID v1 vs body composition divergence

§IID v1 (drafted 2026-05-14): Stiegler / Illich / Frankfurt / 君子不器 (four traditions)
Body composition: Stiegler / Obara / Frankfurt / Illich / Hui (five diagnosticians, no 君子不器)

Three options:

**(A) Revise §IID to align with body**: replace 君子不器 with Obara + Hui; §IID becomes 5-tradition convergence matching body. Aligns §IID with the analytical work in §VI-3 and Ch-5-5. Cost: 君子不器 anchor (non-Western, anthropological-ontological) is lost from §IID; user previously endorsed its inclusion (2026-05-14 Q6 walk-through).

**(B) Keep §IID as drafted; conclusion §4.4 grounds in body's 5, not §IID's 4**: §IID becomes a parallel diagnostic anchor at the §II level (with its specific four-tradition convergence including 君子不器 for non-Western breadth); the body's five-diagnostician composition is the canonical pre-squeeze diagnostic at the §V/§VI level. Conclusion §4.4 cites body's 5. Cost: parallel typologies at §II and §V/§VI may confuse readers; risks the entropic-plan problem.

**(C) Keep §IID; revise it to NAME both compositions**: §IID's four traditions are kept as a specific convergence-claim (with 君子不器 as non-Western anchor); §IID adds a paragraph noting that §V-§VI's body work develops a parallel five-diagnostician set (Obara + Stiegler + Frankfurt + Illich + Hui) at the human-experiential-signature level. Cross-references both ways. Cost: §IID gets longer; risks doubling content.

### Decision 2 — Introduction-5 minor adjustments

Three minor adjustments to Introduction-5 before integration into Intro_v1: (1) "Existential" → "anthropological-ontological"; (2) "Technology" → "artificiality"; (3) "Self-completing tendency" consistent with lock §1.3 + S1-11_v4 §E. These are language-level updates, not structural changes; can be made inline during Intro_v1 drafting.

### Decision 3 — Conclusion future-research scope

Per audit, the conclusion needs to handle three types of content for §4.5:

- **(α) Apparatus-side**: convivial tools / pharmacology / cosmotechnics / regulated governance — *summarize* from body; not new content
- **(β) Human-existential-side**: explicitly deferred to conclusion by Ch-6-6 §4.2; conclusion needs to *develop* with Audrey Tang plurality, Hui cosmotechnics existential register, convivial/pluriverse traditions
- **(γ) Future research**: appropriate technology (Schumacher), Kyosei, deeper non-Western comparative (Ch-3-2 gap), artificial-satoyama — explicitly *flag* as future work, not develop

The earlier conclusion spec conflated (α) (β) (γ). The corrected scope has three modes.
