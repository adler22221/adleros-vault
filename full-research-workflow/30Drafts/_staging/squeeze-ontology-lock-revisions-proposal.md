## Revision 1 — F3 refinement (universe-LAWS stable, universe-MANIFESTATIONS dynamic)

### 1a. Current text

From `20Wiki/squeeze-ontology.md` §2.1 (lines 60–64):

> ### F3. Law-change asymmetry
>
> The laws of the universe (thermodynamic, biological, ecological, geographic) are stable on the timescale of human civilization — legible through long observation, unchanged within a generation. The laws of the metaverse (algorithmic governance, platform logics, recommendation systems, data policies) are deliberately engineered, periodically updated, and often deliberately obscured. The adaptation rate the metaverse demands exceeds the response cycles of biological evolution, cultural transmission, and even legislative deliberation.
>
> Prior squeezes imposed stable new pressures whose contours could eventually be learned. The universe-metaverse squeeze imposes *unstable* new pressures whose instability is itself a design feature.

### 1b. Proposed replacement

> ### F3. Stratified two-environment dynamism with shrinking conventional space
>
> The metaverse-stage stratified two-environment configuration (§1.3) produces a distinctive dynamism pattern across its constituent terms. The configuration is not a binary squeeze with one stable and one unstable pole; it is a configuration of universe-laws, universe-law manifestations, and metaverse-laws each operating with distinct dynamism profiles.
>
> **Universe-laws: transfactually stable.** The laws of the universe — thermodynamic, biological, ecological, geographic, chemical — are stable on the timescale of human civilization at the real level of Bhaskar's stratification: causally-generative structures that hold whether or not they are exercised in any specific event. Universe-laws are not what the metaverse-stage subject contends with as a moving target.
>
> Organisms — humans and other creatures — experience these laws *directly* (gravity acting on the falling body; thermodynamics on the thermoregulating body; chemical-biological laws on metabolism) AND through their manifestations. The F2 incommensurability is at the level of organizing principles, not at the level of which-laws-apply: universe-laws still apply in metaverse-mediated environments; what's incommensurable is which selection criteria are operative.
>
> **Universe-law manifestations: dynamic under anthropogenic disturbance.** The planetary manifestations of universe-laws — at the actual level — are dynamic on civilizational timescales, increasingly produced by human worlds. Climate is changing because of anthropogenic emissions; biosphere composition is being altered through habitat conversion and species translocation; ocean chemistry is shifting through carbon dioxide absorption; atmospheric composition is being engineered through fossil-fuel combustion. The Anthropocene literature (Crutzen + Stoermer 2000; Steffen et al. 2007; Chakrabarty 2009; Zalasiewicz et al.; Haraway 2015; Moore on Capitalocene; Tsing 2015) documents this dynamism. At the metaverse-stage, Aspect B (§1.3) extends this Anthropocene agency through new self-completing means: algorithmic agriculture, IoT environmental monitoring, AI-mediated conservation, smart cities, satellite tracking, drone-based ecology, computational ocean management. The universe-side manifestation field is increasingly metaverse-mediated.
>
> **Metaverse-laws: conventional in origin, causally-binding in operation, with conventional space progressively shrinking.** The laws of the metaverse — 自足完成化 at the meta-law stratum (§V §1); architectural-mechanism claims (Lessig's architecture-as-regulation; Hui's productive-ontologies-via-associated-milieus; recursive retention/protention coupling; §V §2); operationalized through the four minimally sufficient technologies (AI / big data / IoT / code-as-law; §V §3) — are deliberately engineered (conventional in origin: humanly produced, made / decided / created), revisable in principle, often deliberately obscured. They *possess causally-binding power*: once instantiated, they operate transfactually, regardless of ongoing human enactment ("causally-binding" here names compelling-power without restricting to deterministic mechanisms; it accommodates both classical mechanism and probabilistic ML — both are causally-binding in operation). As 自足完成化 progresses, the conventional space — for decision, revision, contestation — progressively shrinks: initial decisions become harder to revise once the system reaches operational maturity; algorithmic systems become entrenched; mutations become path-dependent on prior architectural choices; auditability decreases. Metaverse-laws thus migrate from a dominantly conventional opening to an increasingly closed causally-binding register, transgressing the classical νόμος / φύσις pair: nomos-origin + physis-operation in a single structural fact that the classical pair could not anticipate.
>
> **The asymmetry produces a configuration-wide dynamism, not a binary "double-dynamism."** Each of the three terms moves with its own dynamism profile: universe-laws stable; universe-law manifestations dynamic under anthropogenic and metaverse-extended disturbance; metaverse-laws engineered, with revision-space progressively closing. The metaverse-stage subject — humans acutely, other creatures via Aspect B — adapts to a configuration whose components are moving for structurally different reasons, none of which is the stable fallback substrate that prior squeezes could presume to inhabit.
>
> Prior F1+F2 squeezes (the pre-metaverse Obara-trajectory squeezes for humans; the late-Pleistocene-onward squeezes for many other species) operated against the relatively stable background of universe-law manifestations and a dominantly conventional artificial-side. The metaverse-stage configuration adds (i) dynamism at the universe-law manifestation level under anthropogenic and metaverse-extended disturbance, and (ii) the engineered, progressively-closing artificial-side organizing principles. The double-source of instability is the operational feature of the metaverse-stage F3.

### 1c. Rationale

The original F3 framing treats the universe as a stable background and the metaverse as a singular unstable pole. Both are imprecise. The revised F3 addresses three structural concerns:

First, *empirical accuracy at the universe-side*. The planetary manifestations of universe-laws are demonstrably dynamic on civilizational timescales — climate change, biosphere alteration, ocean chemistry, atmospheric composition, all on documented anthropogenic trajectories now extended through metaverse-stage Aspect B. A lock that treats the universe-side as stable across laws-and-manifestation strata together would be empirically inaccurate.

Second, *precision at the metaverse-side*. Metaverse-laws are NOT simply "unstable" in the sense of arbitrary; they are *conventional in origin* (engineered, made, decided) AND *causally-binding in operation* (compelling, transfactual, self-completing). The conventional-origin opens a revision-space that is *progressively closing* through 自足完成化. This is structurally sharper than "metaverse-side moves" and connects to the classical νόμος / φύσις transgression developed in §1.3.

Third, *consistency with the stratified two-environment configuration* (§1.3). F3 must describe the dynamism of the configuration as a whole, not of a binary squeeze. The revised F3 is anchored on the three terms of the stratified configuration (universe-laws, universe-law manifestations, metaverse-laws) and on the Anthropocene continuation that connects pre-metaverse and metaverse-stage Aspect-B agency. This also answers the committee question "but is the universe really stable across the period the thesis is analyzing?" — universe-LAWS are; universe-law MANIFESTATIONS are not.

Fourth, *organisms experience both laws and manifestations*. The original F3 framing implicitly assumed organisms contend with universe-laws as a moving target; the revised version makes explicit that organisms experience universe-laws directly (gravity, thermodynamics) AND universe-law manifestations (local ecosystems), and that F2 incommensurability is at the organizing-principles level, not at which-laws-apply.

## Revision 2 — Squeeze-vs-collapse distinction

### 2a. Current text

The current squeeze-ontology lock does not distinguish squeeze from collapse. The terms are used in a way that allows conflation: §VI.7's collapse-prospect material would, on the current lock, be ambiguous about whether it is analyzing the same phenomenon as §§VI.1–VI.6 or a different one. The lock §2 references "prior squeezes" without specifying whether prior collapses (e.g., Roman, Maya) count as prior squeezes or prior collapse-endpoints.

### 2b. Proposed addition

Add a new section §1b (immediately after §1 "The squeeze defined") with the following content:

> ### §1b — Squeeze versus collapse: an analytical distinction
>
> The squeeze and collapse are distinct phenomena that must not be conflated.
>
> The squeeze is the *ongoing structural condition* of being jointly subject to incommensurable organizing principles / selection criteria (F1+F2 above), operative at the individual-organism level (Level 1) and at the world-as-system-of-means level (Level 2) — see §1.3. It is a condition that obtains for as long as F1+F2 hold at either or both levels. The squeeze can be lived with — for finite intervals, under specific conditions of recognition and navigation (cf. §3 productive dimension, dependency-turn).
>
> Collapse is what happens at the endpoint when the squeeze becomes irreversible and the squeezed being can no longer sustain dual satisfaction. Collapse is the *crossing of a threshold* beyond which the structural strain breaks the subject's capacity to operate at all. For an organism, collapse means extinction; for a civilization or world (Level 2), collapse means the loss of sociopolitical complexity or the dissolution of the world-as-OS into its now-independent components; for a person, collapse means the breakdown of the dual operation by which one was navigating both environments.
>
> Prior squeezes (animal F1+F2 squeezes since late Pleistocene; human F1+F2 squeezes since Obara's self-domestication onset, intensifying through self-petification) operated at the dominantly conventional register of human-created artificial-ecosystem organizing principles. Prior collapses (civilizational examples — Roman, Maya, Easter Island; ongoing animal extinctions) are collapse-endpoints of these conventional-register squeezes. The metaverse-stage squeeze is distinguished from prior squeezes not by being the first F1+F2 squeeze (it is not) but by (i) the stratified two-environment configuration, in which metaverse-laws constitute a new real-level background environment parallel to the universe; (ii) the conventional-origin + causally-binding-operation register-transgression with progressively shrinking conventional space (per §1.3); and (iii) the two-level squeeze in which worlds-as-systems-of-means are also F1+F2 subjects.
>
> The analytical resources for squeeze and collapse come from different literatures. The squeeze is analyzed through structural-environmental concepts (F1+F2 above; the operational features F3–F5; the bidirectional coupling of 自己完結化 ↔ 自足完成化 specified in §V §1.2; the stratified two-environment configuration in §1.3). Collapse is analyzed through different literatures — Tainter's complexity-maintenance equations, Holling's adaptive cycle, Perrow's normal accidents, Homer-Dixon's tight-coupling synthesis, Taleb's fragility/antifragility, Schwartz and Nichols's regeneration-base claim — each of which addresses the threshold-crossing rather than the structural condition that precedes it.
>
> The distinction matters for two reasons. First, the diagnostic urgency of the squeeze is misread if it is conflated with the diagnostic urgency of collapse. The squeeze can be structurally severe without producing immediate collapse for finite intervals; the squeeze can also approach its collapse endpoint without warning. The distinction is necessary to maintain analytical precision about which claim is being made.
>
> Second, the *thesis's distinctive contribution at the collapse-prospect stratum* — Claim C: the metaverse-stage destroys its own regeneration-base — is structurally distinct from the squeeze claim. The squeeze claim is structural-ontological (F1+F2 at two levels under the stratified two-environment configuration). Claim C is a structural-prognosis at the collapse-endpoint stratum, integrating the Ch-5-5 convergent-diagnosis material with Schwartz and Nichols's regeneration-base concept. Keeping squeeze and collapse analytically separate is what allows the thesis to deliver both claims without conflation.

### 2c. Rationale

The §VI v2 architecture explicitly separates the ongoing-squeeze argument (§§VI.1–VI.6) from the collapse-prospect analysis (§VI.7). Without an explicit squeeze-vs-collapse distinction in the lock, the §VI.7 material would be ambiguously positioned relative to the lock's F1+F2 framework.

The distinction also resolves a tension in the current lock §2 framing. §2 addresses Liu's challenge "那個squeeze跟過去的有何不同?" by differentiating the universe-metaverse squeeze from "prior squeezes" — but the cited prior cases (the Industrial Revolution, urbanization, the communications revolution) are *transitions* of the conventional-register F1+F2 squeeze, not collapse-events. Adding the squeeze-vs-collapse distinction sharpens this: prior F1+F2 squeezes operated at the dominantly conventional register (animal cases since late Pleistocene; human cases since self-domestication); prior collapses are collapse-endpoints of these conventional-register squeezes; the metaverse-stage squeeze is distinguished by the stratified two-environment configuration, register-transgression, and two-level structure (per §1.3) — not by being the first F1+F2 squeeze. The earlier framing's "within-environment human squeezes" qualifier is dropped: pre-metaverse human squeezes were already between-environment squeezes (F1+F2 between universe-originated and human-created artificial ecosystems), just at a different register and configuration. Liu's challenge is answered against both prior conventional-register F1+F2 squeezes and prior collapses.

### 2d. Integration location

Insert as new §1b immediately after `20Wiki/squeeze-ontology.md` §1 (lines 24–47) and before §2 (line 49). No replacement of existing text; this is additive. Cross-references in §6 (line 240) gain new entries for §VI.7 deploying §1b's distinction.

## Revision 3 — Stratified two-environment configuration, two-level squeeze, and species-relativity (full rewrite, supersedes earlier 3b)

### 3a. Current text and what it gets wrong

The squeeze-ontology lock §1 currently frames F1+F2 in terms that implicitly assume (i) the human as the squeezed subject, (ii) the metaverse-stage as the moment that initiates the human squeeze, and (iii) the squeeze as a binary condition between "universe" and "metaverse" as undifferentiated poles. Three structural inadequacies follow:

- **Pre-metaverse human squeeze is missed.** The F2 incommensurability between *universe-originated ecosystems* and *human-created artificial ecosystems* applies to both humans and other creatures continuously — not initiated by the metaverse. Pre-metaverse human-created artificial ecosystems (institutions, supply chains, infrastructural systems, agricultural practices, market structures) have their own organizing principles incommensurable with universe-originated ecosystems. Humans have always-already been F1+F2 squeezed alongside other creatures since Obara's self-domestication onset.

- **The "universe-laws vs. metaverse-laws" framing elides the stratified ontology.** Organisms experience both universe-laws directly (gravity, thermodynamics, biological-chemical laws) AND their manifestations (local ecosystems, climate, terrain, food web, pathogen spectrum). The F2 incommensurability operates at the level of *organizing principles / selection criteria*, not at the level of which-laws-apply (gravity applies in cities; thermodynamics applies in factories). The original framing missed this.

- **The structural novelty of the metaverse-stage requires a richer specification than "the artificial side ascends to the laws stratum."** Pre-metaverse human-created artificial ecosystems already have their own real-level generative structures (institutions, supply chains, infrastructural systems). What is genuinely new at the metaverse-stage is the emergence of a *second real-level background environment* with engineered laws — the stratified two-environment configuration developed below — and the structural reorganization of the squeeze that this entails.

### 3b. Proposed addition

Add to `20Wiki/squeeze-ontology.md` §1 a clarification clause after the existing F2 paragraphs:

> ### §1.3 — Structural form, species-and-OS relativity, and the metaverse-stage condition
>
> F1+F2 specify a structural condition that is **neither species-specific nor specific to the metaverse-stage**. The condition obtains whenever a subject (organism, or world-as-OS) is jointly constrained by two environments whose organizing principles (selection criteria, fitness measures, niche-organizing logics) are not mutually translatable. Both pre-metaverse cases and the metaverse-stage are instances of F1+F2; what differs is the specific filling of the two environments, the register of the artificial-side organizing principles, and — at the metaverse-stage — the stratified two-environment configuration described below.
>
> **Ideal-type usage.** Throughout this revision and the lock generally, "world" and "mode of artificiality" are used as Weberian heuristic-comparative constructs (ideal types) — not Platonic forms, not normative ideals, not Husserlian essences. Each world (as ideal type) is built upon its own ideal-typical mode of artificiality (the artifice-making subset of its collective survival / adaptational strategies). This usage is heuristic and analytical; it commits to no essentialist or normative reading.
>
> #### What organisms experience
>
> Organisms — humans and other creatures alike — experience both universe-laws directly (gravity acting on the falling body; thermodynamics on the thermoregulating body; biological-chemical laws on metabolism) AND universe-law manifestations (local ecosystems, climate, terrain, food web, pathogen spectrum, the historical-geographic conditions that produce the lived environment). Both layers are operative; the F2 incommensurability is at the level of organizing principles / selection criteria, not at the level of which-laws-apply.
>
> #### Always-existing squeeze (humans + other creatures, since the relevant niche-construction transitions)
>
> The F2 incommensurability between *universe-originated ecosystems* and *human-created artificial ecosystems* applies continuously:
>
> - **Other-creatures case**: universe-originated niche manifestations (predator behavior, plant chemistry, climate, native pathogens, landscape connectivity, reproductive phenology) ⇄ human-created artificial-ecosystem manifestations (human proximity, crop/refuse food sources, traps and firearms, roads and fences, light/noise/chemical pollution, climate-altered conditions). The structural form has been the operative condition for many non-human species for at least fifty thousand years; the empirical record is the record of anthropogenic extinction (cf. §VI.2), now situated within the broader Anthropocene literature (Crutzen + Stoermer 2000; Steffen et al. 2007; Chakrabarty 2009; Zalasiewicz et al.; Haraway 2015; Moore on Capitalocene; Tsing 2015 on multispecies).
>

These dynamics constitute the human pre-metaverse F1+F2 squeeze; the metaverse-stage inherits, rather than initiates, it.
>
> Pre-metaverse human-created artificial ecosystems are NOT "merely manifestations under universe-laws." They have their own real-level generative structures (institutions, supply chains, infrastructural systems, agricultural practices, market structures), with a **dominantly conventional register**: those structures are practice-enacted, contestable, and persist through ongoing human performance, with causally-binding margins (legal coercion enforced by state monopoly on violence; infrastructural lock-in; market compulsion) that do not dominate the register.
>
> #### The new condition at the metaverse-stage — the stratified two-environment configuration
>
> What is structurally new at the metaverse-stage is the emergence of a *second real-level background environment* (the metaverse) parallel to but incommensurable with the universe. The metaverse-laws — the real-level stratum of the metaverse — comprise the meta-law of self-completion (Yang's 自足完成化, extending Uegaki's 自己完結化: the disposition of artificiality to become self-sufficient, capable of operating and maintaining itself without ongoing human direction) and the architectural-mechanism claims developed in §V (Lessig's architecture-as-regulation; Hui's productive-ontologies-via-associated-milieus; the recursive coupling of tertiary retention and tertiary protention).
>
> The metaverse-stage condition is constituted by two structurally-inseparable aspects:
>
> **Aspect A — Metaverse-laws as a new term.** A new kind of law enters the system: **conventional in origin** (made / decided / created / engineered by humans — nomos register) **AND causally-binding in operation** (compelling, transfactual, self-completing without ongoing human enactment — physis-operation register). This transgresses the classical νόμος / φύσις pair: nomos-origin + physis-operation in a single structural fact that the classical pair could not anticipate. Metaverse-laws thus begin in the conventional register (decided, designed, written) but migrate toward an increasingly closed causally-binding register where the conventional revision-space disappears.

> **Aspect B — What metaverse-laws DO to the universe-side terms.** Metaverse-laws act on the field of universe-law manifestations that organisms inhabit. The specific operations include [terms pending author endorsement; Claude's working placeholders are "mediation" (navigation apps standing between humans and spatial experience; algorithmic agriculture standing between livestock and feeding regimes; satellite tracking standing between wildlife migration and data), "overlay" (data streams laid on top of physical environments; sensor networks over ecosystems; AI-mediated conservation management over wildlife habitats), and "hybridization" (smart cities reshaping urban wildlife; algorithmic agriculture transforming livestock conditions; environmental sensing redefining wildlife observation; platform-mediated human social environments)]. >

Without B, A is merely a parallel laws-system; without A, B is merely intensified manifestation-level competition. The structural novelty is constituted by both together.
>
> #### Two-level squeeze
>
> Under the metaverse-stage condition, F1+F2 applies at two structurally distinct but mechanistically coupled levels:
>
> - **Level 1 — Organisms-as-individuals squeezed.** Individual humans (acutely) and other creatures (increasingly, via Aspect B) face incommensurable selection criteria from the universe-originated and artificial-side environments. For humans, this is the Obara-style petification squeeze whose psychosomatic / existential / livelihood-precarity manifestations §VI(A) develops. For other creatures, this is the Anthropocene's structural agency on wildlife, livestock, marine life, and ecosystems generally, now extended through metaverse-stage Aspect B (algorithmic agriculture, smart-city infrastructure, environmental sensing, satellite tracking, AI-mediated conservation).
>
> - **Level 2 — Worlds-as-systems-of-means squeezed.** The *world itself* — as an OS-specific-to-anthropological-humans constituted by a configuration of *means* (institutions, technologies, practices, infrastructures, social arrangements) — is now also squeezed between universe and metaverse. The world diminishes as its components migrate from "internal means" to "background environment": pre-metaverse the means are internal to the world and serve human purposes; through the bidirectional coupling of 自己完結化 (Uegaki, human-side delegation) and 自足完成化 (Yang, apparatus-side self-completion), the means progressively gain operational independence; once they self-complete sufficiently they cease to be internal means and constitute a *background environment of their own* — the metaverse. The world's own components leave the world and become its pressure. The world contracts; the 役割 humans play in the world shrinks (cf. §V §1.2). The world is now squeezed between the universe (always-prior background) and the metaverse (newly-emergent background, constituted by what was previously the world's own means).
>
> Level 1 and Level 2 are mechanistically coupled. The individual-level squeeze (Level 1) drives the world-diminishing dynamic (Level 2) via 自己完結化: as individuals delegate to means, the means become independent, the world contracts. Conversely, as the world contracts, individuals find themselves in a thinner world whose means have migrated to its background, intensifying their Level-1 squeeze. The coupling is bidirectional and self-reinforcing, structurally identical to the 自己完結化 ↔ 自足完成化 bidirectional coupling at the structural-OS level.
>
> F1 (between-structure) thus applies at Level 1 (organisms between universe and metaverse manifestation fields) AND at Level 2 (worlds between universe and metaverse as background environments). F2 (incommensurability) applies at both levels: incommensurable selection criteria for organisms; incommensurable structural pressures for worlds.
>
> #### Environmentalist-stratified, not organicist-fused
>
> The metaverse-laws constitute a new *real-level background environment with its own laws*, NOT a Hui-style organizing-inorganic superorganism that fuses apparatus and environment. The thesis keeps both poles real: the universe remains a real-level background environment with its own transfactual laws indifferent to data-generativity; the metaverse becomes a *new* real-level background environment with its self-completing tendency selecting for data-generativity. Humans (and other creatures via Aspect B) are caught not inside a single superorganism but between two incommensurable real-level environments. This commitment is methodologically critical: the stratified two-environment configuration is articulable only if both poles remain real.
>
> #### What §1.3 does NOT cover: the §III-8 / §VI boundary
>
> §III-8 establishes that (i) the modern Western mode of artificiality is one of many modes; (ii) modes are *collective strategies of adaptation / survival*; (iii) modes can fail when pressured by the universe or its planetary manifestations; (iv) this applies to both human worlds AND animal operating systems. The F1+F2 lock developed here specifies the *structural form* of the squeeze that, in its metaverse-stage stratified-two-environment-configuration instantiation, characterizes why the modern Western mode at the metaverse-stage represents a structurally novel vulnerability — the conventionally-originated-but-causally-binding-in-operation register with progressively shrinking conventional space, plus Aspect B's reorganization of the universe-side manifestation field. §III-8 ↔ §VI thus operate complementarily: §III-8 establishes modal pluralism + the universal mode-failure-under-pressure point; §VI specifies the structural-form analysis of the metaverse-stage's unique configuration. Neither replaces the other; the prior framing that treated §III-8 as a "single-environment failure" foil to §VI's "two-environment squeeze" is dropped.

### 3c. Rationale

The revised Revision 3 addresses six structural concerns surfaced in the session-2026-05-12 dialogue:

First, *empirical and conceptual accuracy about the pre-metaverse human condition*. The F2 incommensurability between universe-originated and human-created artificial ecosystems applies to humans pre-metaverse — Obara's self-domestication → self-petification trajectory is precisely the empirical record of the pre-metaverse human F1+F2 squeeze.
