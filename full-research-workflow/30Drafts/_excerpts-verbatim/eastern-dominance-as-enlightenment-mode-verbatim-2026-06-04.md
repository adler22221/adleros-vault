# Eastern Dominance as the Enlightenment MODE Wearing an Eastern Face — Verbatim Dossier

**Thesis claim served:** "Eastern dominance" (the global rise of China / East Asia) is NOT evidence of an alternative to the Western/Enlightenment *mode* of artificiality/technicity. It is largely the **universalization / extension of that same mode** — the *mode* of techno-modernity universalizes even if the *region* (the West) declines. Catching up with, or overtaking, the West does not by itself constitute an alternative to the technological condition.

## 1. YUK HUI  — primary target (richest)

Hui is the strongest single voice for the "mode-not-region" claim. He argues that **modernization = the adoption of European techno-science**, producing a single globalized (European-derived) technical system ("monotechnology"); that East Asia / China has **synchronized** to Western modernity and its linear time; that this *erodes* Chinese cosmotechnics (the Needham-question framing, "disorientation," "China ceases to be China"); and that **technodiversity** is needed *precisely because* the non-West did not escape the universal technical mode but adopted it — so that merely catching up technologically is no alternative.
