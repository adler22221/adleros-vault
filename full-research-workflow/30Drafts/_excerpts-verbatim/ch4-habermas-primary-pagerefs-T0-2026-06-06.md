## B3 — A&H expand instrumental reason / reification back to hominization
**Dossier ref:** *TCA* 1:366/489 (some secondary cite p.489) — via Zuidervaart p.110.
**PRIMARY CONFIRMATION:** ✅ Located in TCA Vol 1 primary, PDF page 409, which carries the printed running head **"366 REASON AND THE RATIONALIZATION OF SOCIETY"** (verified via page-edge extraction). [VERBATIM-PRIMARY: TCA1 | p.366 | "Horkheimer and Adorno see themselves forced ... to sink the foundations of the reification critique still deeper and to expand instrumental reason into a category of the world-historical process of civilization as a whole, that is, to project the process of reification back behind the capitalist beginnings of the modern age into the very beginnings of hominization. With this, however, the contours of the concept of reason are in danger of becoming blurred."]

**PRIMARY CONFIRMATION:** ✅ Located in TCA Vol 1 primary, PDF page 423, running head **"380 REASON AND THE RATIONALIZATION OF SOCIETY"**. Dossier's **1:380 is CORRECT**.
