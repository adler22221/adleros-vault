# Concept Genealogy (CORRECTED) — for re-founding the WYoE Vol 29 proposal
> **AI draft (Phase 1, rev. 2).** Earlier version wrongly rooted the system in synthetic anthropology — corrected here against the actual 2018 source documents. **Verified** = read this session. **Pending** = current `.gdoc` not yet read (四大危機, Education Bypass Surgery).
> **Correction of record:** 総合人間学会 (Japan Assoc. of Synthetic Anthropology) is the *venue* where allocation dependence was presented and published (2021–). It is **not** the conceptual origin. The origin is 2018 educational praxis, out of the documentary *If There Is a Reason to Study* and the Awakening NPO.

## 1. The corrected evolution arc

**Stage 0 — 2018 roots (educational praxis, from the documentary + NPO). [Verified]**
- **In Search of Modes of Coexistence for a Healthy, Sustainable, and Sincere World** (May 2018). The master concept: *"All problems or sufferings… derive from our relationships with the world around us — in other words, human's **modes of coexistence**."* Education is defined as **"the process of forming the individual's modes of coexistence with the rest of the world"** — the constituents being *ourselves, other beings, technologies, knowledge, societies, and **nature***. Vision: a *healthy, sustainable, sincere* world. → the constructive root + the planetary definition of education. `[[In Search of Modes of Coexistence]]` *(G:\ …Modes of Coexistence; current gdoc read 2026-05-31)*
- **The Linear Process of Exhaustion** (2018). The diagnostic root: a *funneling model* — schooling sorts children by abstraction-capability into the powerful's sense of "usefulness/function," and learners de-prioritize self-actualization to fit the system (loss of innocence), explicitly tied to *"the world crises."* `[[The Linear Process of Exhaustion]]`
- **Learning by Contributing (創中學)** — the pedagogical predecessor: *"from dependence/exhaustion-based competition (依存・損耗的競争) to reciprocal/regenerative coexistence (互恵・再生的共生)."* Curiosity + compassion → action for others → reciprocation → resources reused for further contribution. Tested with formerly-marginalized youth (non-attenders, single-parent, precariat) who gained agency, entered selective universities without entrance exams, founded social enterprises, won international awards. `[[ラーニング・バイ・コントリビューティング]]`
- **四大危機 (four crises)** and **Education Bypass Surgery (教育繞道手術)** — the crisis frame + the intervention model. *[current gdocs Pending]*

**Stage 1 — Diagnostic specialization → allocation dependence (2021). [Verified]**
Modes of coexistence, examined in education, specialize into modes of *dependence*. The discovery paper (「平等」を求めることに「消耗」… 配分依存の発見): agents *dependent on resource allocation* are forced to fit allocation criteria → **assimilation (同化) + pruning (刈込み)**; a shift from *"intrinsically valuable acts" to "monetary acts"*; a fragility that "runs **from human society to the natural environment**." Method: **critical realism + retroduction**; case: his alternative school (人文); data: the documentary (Yang 2016) + interviews; grounded in Eastern-medicine holism (天人合一; 上医治未病). `[[「平等」を求めることに「消耗」配分依存の発見]]` · `[[Allocation Dependence 配分依存]]`

**Stage 2 — The dependence turn + modes of dependencies. [Verified]**
Class (Marx) → power (Weber/Foucault) → **dependence** ("dependence makes power powerful," 無欲則剛). Allocation generalizes into a family: 配分 / 貨幣 (money) / 理性 (rationality) / later 制御性 (controllability/tech). `[[dependence turn]]`

**Stage 3 — Generative counter-mechanism → learning by caring (2022 CR hinge). [Verified]**
LbC = the **positive evaluation** (generative mechanism, *"theory of transition from dependence to reciprocity / metaReality"*) following the **negative evaluation** (explanatory critique, Bhaskar) of allocation dependence. Learning by Contributing (創中學) → **Learning by Caring (德中學)**: care, not contribution, as the self-organizing principle; rooted in Mencius 求其放心, Daoist 無待, Buddhist dependent origination. `[[202207010253 Learning By Caring CR Abstract]]` · A clean definition (`[[202103252119 Why a human economy]]`): LbC *"taps the power of reciprocity… so the resources [learners] receive… alleviate their dependence on the measures of impersonal resource allocation."*

**Stage 4 — Institutional turn (2023–24). [Mapped]** Prefigurative academia / academia-as-hub / experimental higher education; education as web/weaving (編織).

**Stage 5 — Planetary convergence (2024–26). [Verified for 2026-05-22]** **Education AS Sustainable Development** (Yang & Pi 2023) + the 2026-05-22 synthesis foregrounding LbC's planetary payoffs. `[[2026-05-22 World yearbook of Education]]`

## 2. The planetary through-line (load-bearing)

1. **Master framing (Stage 0):** education = forming our modes of coexistence with the world *including nature* → planetary crisis **is** a crisis of those modes, and education is "both implicated in and potentially transformative of planetary futures" (the CfP's own words). Strongest, most on-brief hook.
2. **Allocation dependence runs to the biosphere:** fragility "from human society to the natural environment" (discovery paper).
3. **Adaptive resource use** (2026-05-22): *"the act of care can happen in any circumstances by utilizing whatever is at hand"* — quality decoupled from material throughput (equality + scarcity-resilience).
4. **Care as AI-/metric-proof value-creation**; **care → social cohesion → counter to polarization** (speaks to §2).
5. **Honest material limit:** livelihood needs (food/energy/housing) + the monetary threshold — no techno-utopianism.

## 4. Re-founded spine (Phase 2 recommendation — for Adler to confirm)
1. **Frame:** education = the forming of our modes of coexistence with the world (nature included); planetary crisis = a crisis of those modes.
2. **Diagnosis:** modern schooling is dominated by one pathological mode — **allocation dependence** (assimilation + pruning; the Linear Process of Exhaustion) — yielding productivist, resource-hungry, metric-driven education whose fragility reaches the biosphere.
3. **Counter-mode:** **Learning by Caring (德中學)** — agent-based, adaptive, AI-/metric-proof, equality-preserving; reciprocity that *reduces* allocation dependence (from Learning by Contributing 創中學).
4. **Horizon:** **Education AS Sustainable Development** — institutions that live the regenerative coexistence they cultivate (Yang & Pi 2023).
5. **Method:** retroductive critical realism + autoethnography (the documentary) + practitioner action research (the Lab); causal power of LbC over heroic intervention.
6. **Contribution:** a non-Western relational ontology the planetary-education conversation lacks; a structural account of educational climate inaction; a regenerative, AI-era-proof pathway. Single-Taiwan grounding → global problematic. **Section: §3.**
