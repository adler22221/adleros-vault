> The pandemic-driven move to videoconferencing made one specific form of this body-as-not-as-willed visible at scale. *Zoom dysmorphia*, named in dermatology and psychological literature from 2020 onward (Rice et al. 2020; Pikoos et al. 2021), describes the heightened dissatisfaction with one's own facial appearance produced by repeated exposure to one's image in video calls — an exposure that asymmetrically magnifies features the subject sees in no other condition of embodied life. ## Ch-4-6 — Expand transhumanism case differentiation

**Status**: PARTIAL. Signal-suppression passage present verbatim at P0163. Mind-uploading critique integrated. The intermediate cases (longevity, restorative prosthesis, cognitive enhancement, germline) lack explicit differentiation.

**Placement**: P0161–P0164 (transhumanism section in Ch IV).

**Suggested 2-paragraph expansion (~300w):**

> The transhumanist program is not uniform, and its varied cases stand in different relations to the squeeze. *Longevity research* targets the body's metabolic decline; insofar as it succeeds, it extends the period during which the universe-side body remains a viable substrate for life-as-willed pursuits. It does not, in itself, deepen the squeeze. *Restorative prosthesis* (limb replacement after injury, cochlear implants, retinal prostheses) restores capacities the universe-side body has lost; it operates within the existing endosomatic/exosomatic complementarity rather than reorganizing it. *Cognitive enhancement* (pharmacological, neural interface) is more ambiguous: insofar as it expands what the universe-side body can do, it extends rather than displaces; insofar as it reorganizes cognition to meet metaverse-side demands more efficiently, it intensifies the squeeze by deepening the apparatus-side coupling. *Mind-uploading* — the speculative endpoint of consciousness-substrate transfer — would represent the terminal articulation of the squeeze: the abandonment of the universe-side substrate altogether in favor of pure metaverse-side existence.

> *Germline editing* operates at a different level still — not a prosthetic intervention on a living subject but a reorganization of the developmental conditions of subsequent subjects. Its squeeze-implications are mediated by what those future subjects will inherit: an endosomatic constitution shaped to better fit the apparatus they will encounter. The case sits at the boundary between endosomatic evolution (under altered selection criteria) and exosomatic engineering (artificiality reaching back into the developmental substrate). The thesis's account of the squeeze does not predict which way any specific transhumanist program turns; it specifies the structural axis along which each case can be located.

## Ch-5-6 — Develop transindividuation as alternative to dividuation

**Status**: PARTIAL. Disruption-as-nihilistic-business-model present at P0179–P0183. Dividuation (Stiegler/Deleuze) present. *Transindividuation* mentioned in one sentence reference; underdeveloped.

**Placement**: P0182–P0183 (algorithmic governmentality section in Ch V).

**Suggested 1-paragraph expansion (~250w):**

> Against algorithmic governmentality's address-of-dividuals, Stiegler counterposes *transindividuation*: the psychic-and-collective individuation by which subjects come to themselves through shared technical-symbolic mediation. Drawing on Simondon (1958, 2020), transindividuation names the structural condition under which individual psychic life and collective social life develop through one another — neither prior to the other, both produced through the technical milieu that mediates them. The dividuating apparatus operates on extracted data profiles below the level at which psychic-and-collective individuation is constituted; transindividuation, where it occurs, operates at and above that level by sustaining the conditions of inheritance, deliberation, and meaning-making across generations. The contrast is not one of slogan-versus-counter-slogan but of operative levels: algorithmic governmentality intervenes in the calculable substratum of individuation; transindividuation requires the longer time-horizons of cultural retention and the deliberative-relational scales at which psychic-collective formations consolidate. Stiegler's prescriptive register, where it appears in his late work (2018; 2020), is the call to develop technical and institutional forms that sustain the transindividuating function under conditions where the dividuating apparatus has captured most of the technical milieu — a call this thesis registers without committing to its specific programmatic forms.
