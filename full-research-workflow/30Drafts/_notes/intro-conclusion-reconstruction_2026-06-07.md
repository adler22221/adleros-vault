> Markup artifact (not prose). ## The architecture in one line
**Flight from dependence (the disease) → the squeeze → the turn toward dependence (the cure)**, with the **dependency turn at Level 0** as the pivot. The sovereign subject (Ch IV) denies dependence and offloads the anti-intended; that flight produces the squeeze (Ch VI); the dependency turn — *investigating what we, as embedded beings, depend on* — is the way out. This single arc runs through Introduction, Chapter VI, and Conclusion.

2. **Rigorous** engagement of the AI-succession / thought-being scenario — in the **Conclusion** (substrate-contingency; the thought-being is a *wish, not a species*; the squeeze is not anthropocentric).
3. The constructive **openings live in the Conclusion**; Chapter VI only gestures.
4. The **dependency turn (依存転回) is the Level-0 spine** — the *stance* of the inquiry (Intro), the ground of §VI.7 (Ch VI), the organizing question of the Conclusion. The *turn* is foundational; the five-mode dependency *taxonomy* stays minimal/background (anti-overwhelm).

## INTRODUCTION — reconstructed skeleton

**I.0 — The puzzle and the paradox (hook).**
- The mistranslation 元宇宙 ("original universe") and Meta's capture of the term; the 2022 stake — definitions set the boundary of imaginable futures; "reclaim futures from the capitalists" (keep; current Intro P0012).
- 【THREAD: Meta ▸ plant】 The paradox: by 2026 the metaverse is widely written off as a failed fad — Meta's losses, the mocked VR, the pivot to AI — yet this thesis argues it has barely begun, *because what failed was never the metaverse*. 【VERIFY: Reality Labs cumulative loss figure (~$70B)】

**I.1 — Why the mainstream definitions fail (three diagnoses).** (Keep current A/B/C; sharpen.)
- (A) Path-dependency on the arbitrary "beyond-universe" origin → fails to grasp the metaverse's disposition. 【THREAD: Meta ▸ plant, cont.】 The XR definition has been *empirically refuted* by the trajectory of the technologies it tried to encompass; what actually arrived — self-completing artificiality (AI, big data, IoT, code-as-law) — is the real metaverse, arriving unnamed. 【REUSE: Intro-sim §1A】
- (B) Disciplinary reductionism (Montuori).
- (C) **Actualistic** inquiry (CR grounding: conflating real/actual/empirical). 【DECISION: adopt the "Actualistic" rename from "Positivistic"? — recommend yes; consistent with 道/理/相. REUSE: Intro-sim §1C】
- The strategic opening: Meta's failure broke the capitalist capture of the term; the thesis reclaims the definition.

**I.2 — The thesis's stance: investigating what we depend on.** 【SPINE: dependency turn ▸ plant / Level 0】
- The reorientation: rather than ask *is artificiality a threat?* or *how do we control it?* — the questions of power, ownership, and liberty — this thesis asks the prior question: **what do we, as embedded beings, depend on, and what becomes of those dependencies as the metaverse emerges?**
- This *is* the dispositional, non-positivist method (real-level causal powers, not actualized specimens): investigating dependence = investigating the real-level conditions of embedded existence.
- 【MINIMAL】 State the *turn*, not the modes-taxonomy. 【PRUNE: replace the heavy "Developmental Emergent Realism" naming (Intro-sim §5) with this lighter stance; keep only the 3×3 道/理/相 × 宇宙/世界/偽境 table if a method figure is wanted.】

**I.3 — The three-term ontology (preliminary definitions).** 【REUSE: Intro-sim §2, updated】
- **universe** (realist given; precedes us; uncontrollable) / **world** (the AOS *buffer*, nested, idealist) / **metaverse** (偽境, the second given — "materially dependent on, yet irreducible to, the universe").
- **human ≠ *Homo sapiens* (ヒト)** — the embedded, coupled being (load-bearing for Ch VI's cast).
- **artificiality** (technology / virtuality / laws of artificial environments).
- The metaverse definition is *built up* dispositionally, not committed early — it *is* the structural condition, not a class of technologies.

**I.4 — The central claim: the squeeze, and what it is beneath.**
- State the **squeeze hypothesis**: human existence squeezed between the universe and the metaverse as the world (the buffer) is hollowed; the **two engines** — the self-finishing of human life (Ch IV) and the self-completion of artificiality (Ch V), two sides of one coin.
- 【THREAD: three traditions ▸ plant】 The thesis works *beneath* law (Montesquieu), labor (Marx), and liberty (Mill–Berlin) — and "beneath" means the **level of dependence**: those traditions presuppose the sovereign agent and ask how to protect or empower it; the thesis asks the prior dependency question the sovereign frame forecloses. *The structural-ontological level beneath law, labor, and liberty is the level of what embedded beings depend on.* 【ACQUIRE: Mill 1859, Berlin 1958 — not in Zotero】 【REUSE: Intro-sim §4, compressed to a signpost】
- 【THREAD: liberation/two-faces ▸ plant】 The liberty point is special: freedom is not the axis. The offloading is felt as *more* as-intendedness, not less freedom; freedom and the threat are two faces of one coin. (Formally free, substantively hollowed — full version in C.1.)
- 【THREAD: Sci-Fi ▸ plant, brief】 And the threat needs no sentient, malicious AI; that frame is set aside here, refuted rigorously in the Conclusion.

**I.5 — The roadmap (chapter previews — updated to the evolved arc + as-intended).** 【REUSE: Intro-sim §6, rewritten】

## CONCLUSION — reconstructed skeleton

**C.0 — What the thesis argued (recap).** 【REUSE: Conclusion-sim §0, updated to the evolved arc】
- Brief six-chapter recap; update Ch IV (life-as-intended / offloading), Ch V (self-completion / 偽道), Ch VI (the four-type evolution, not the old four-cell/three-sign version).
- The squeeze obtains in graded form *now* (causal efficacy precedes completion — answers the "fully actualized?" question).

**C.1 — The threat, reframed: beneath law, labor, and liberty.** 【THREAD: three traditions ▸ harvest】【REUSE: Conclusion-sim §1–§2, updated】
- Not Montesquieuan (governance; GDPR/DMA), not Marxist (ownership; data cooperatives), not Mill–Berlinian (liberty). Each *necessary but structurally insufficient*.
- The "beneath" = the **level of dependence**: each tradition presupposes the agent and asks how to secure its power/property/liberty; the squeeze obtains at the level of what the agent *depends on*. (Unifies with I.2.)
- 【THREAD: liberation/two-faces ▸ harvest】 Mill–Berlin: formally free, substantively hollowed — freedom isn't being taken; the threat is the loss of the *means to survive and adapt* (the buffer hollowed; Claim C — the regeneration base hollowed in advance).

**C.2 — Not the Sci-Fi threat: why the AI-succession scenario fails.** 【THREAD: Sci-Fi/AI ▸ harvest, rigorous】【UPGRADE Conclusion-sim §1's brief set-aside】
- The threat needs no sentient or malicious AI; the structural survival-pressure is already here and sufficient.
- The rigorous refutation: the thought-being living "only in the metaverse, stacked on the universe" is a **wish, not a coming species**. Substrate-contingency — the stack must be maintained from below; giving AI organs in the universe *re-embeds* it (a body, a 道, a squeeze of its own); the squeeze is **not anthropocentric** (any substrate-dependent meaning-builder inherits it). Sentience ≠ self-maintenance; "evolution → mind" is a teleology the thesis resists (the progress trap).
- 【SPINE】 This *is* the flight-from-dependence refuted — the deepest threat is not AI replacing us but *us* hollowing toward the condition of an apparatus and calling it freedom.

**C.3 — The reframe: the dependency turn (Level 0).** 【SPINE: dependency turn ▸ pivot】
- The pivot, stated plainly: the disease is the flight from dependence (the sovereign subject → the squeeze); the cure is the turn toward it.
- The question the thesis hands forward: **what do we, as embedded beings, depend on — and how might we reorganize those dependencies?**
- This unifies C.2 (you cannot flee dependence) and C.4 (so reorganize it). 【MINIMAL: the turn, not the modes-taxonomy; flag source-manuscript, do not cite.】

**C.4 — Where the openings lie: dependency-reorganization.** 【THREAD: openings ▸ harvest — the four levels under Level 0】【REUSE: Conclusion-sim §3 directions + chVI-sim_v3 §VI.7 levels】
- Frame: **harmony = squeeze-prevention** (a world *can* be squeezed, not *must*).
- **Level 0** — the dependency turn (the ground).
- **Level 1** — our dependence on the as-intended / our stance toward the anti-intended → claim less, offload less: 無我, 齊物, Uegaki's 有限の生, 無為.
- **Level 2** — our dependence on world and universe (天地) → re-embed: Kōmoto's 復興のデザイン as *positive niche construction*, permaculture, Illich's conviviality, kyōsei 共生, the pluriverse; preserve/rebuild the regeneration base.
- **Level 3** — our dependence on the made → non-totalizing tools: appropriate technology, Hui's technodiversity / noodiversity.
- **Level 4** — our dependence on the anticipatory apparatus → guide tertiary protention: Audrey Tang's plurality / "pact of care."
- 【open problem】 Deceleration has no evident mechanism yet — named honestly, not resolved.
- These are *dependency-reorganizations*, not fixes.

**C.5 — The generative question, and the reflexive turn.** 【THREAD: Homo-origin ▸ harvest】【REUSE: Conclusion-sim §3 meta-verse turn / current DOCX P0852】
- The Homo-origin question returns: the world was born (Ch I) as a squeeze's resolution — a mediating dependency-structure between biology and a new habitat. **Can a new world be built between the universe and the metaverse** — a new buffer, a re-organized dependence?
- The reflexive turn: to *meta-verse* is to step back and self-reflect; the thesis itself enacts the dependency turn. (Ties to Ch VI Level 4 — re-opening contingency against the metaverse's preemptive determination.)

**C.6 — Closing: the open future and the pharmakon.** 【REUSE: Conclusion-sim §5 / current DOCX P0851】
- Nonlinear open systems; the *pharmakon* (Stiegler) — medicine and poison; recognition is the precondition of reorganization; the work that remains belongs to the inhabitants.
- 【optional, compressed】 a brief standing-of-positions / limits-of-the-analytic note (Conclusion-sim §4) addressing the claim-derivability concern.
