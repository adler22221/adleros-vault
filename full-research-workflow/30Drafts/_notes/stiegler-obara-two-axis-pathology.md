# Stiegler ⇄ Obara: the two-axis pathology of the squeeze
> For **Ch V/VI** (the squeeze's pathology), NOT Ch II — Ch II §E keeps only the *seed* (the ヒト/モノ ズレ). All Stiegler verbatim NLM-verified 2026-06-02 (pages flagged `[VERIFY]` where the late works gave no locator).

## Thesis
The harm the metaverse squeeze inflicts runs on **two coupled axes**, and the thesis needs both because each of its two great interlocutors supplies only one — and routes it one-directionally:
- **Obara** — *first-order biological mismatch* (the **universe**-side: the evolved body directly violated by the artificial environment → the ズレ).
- **Stiegler** — *noetic disindividuation* (the **world**-side: the made meaning-space short-circuiting psychic/collective individuation → loss of attention, desire, knowledge).
- **The thesis** — holds both poles **and theorises the engineered loop between them** that neither thinker closes.

## 1. The contrast — same body, opposite primacy
The difference is **not** presence vs. absence of biological pathology (Stiegler names junk food, circadian disruption, even industrial toxins). It is **direction and primacy**.

- **Stiegler subordinates the biological to the libidinal-noetic.** His pathology is *disindividuation / proletarianisation / symbolic misery*: the technical/programming milieu captures tertiary retention and short-circuits individuation, destroying attention and desire. When the body enters, it enters **downstream** — as the attentional/neurological apparatus captured ("proletarianisation of the nervous system"), and as metabolic degradation that is the *consequence* of a hijacked libidinal economy ("the biological substrata… are themselves a market"). Arrow: **noetic capture → biological degradation.**
- **Obara makes the biological mismatch first-order.** The persisting biological nature (内なる自然 / ヒト) is *directly* violated by the artificial environment — the capsule, the loss of 野生, the self-domesticating "feed" — producing psychophysical and civilisational pathology *as such*, with no detour through hijacked desire. Arrow: **environmental mismatch → pathology.**

This is the pathological face of the "weight and direction" difference (Stiegler exteriorises the human's qualities into technics; Obara holds the irreducible interior). See corrected `chII-sim_v5.md` §B.

## 2. Where the latest evidence falls — it splits
- **Evolutionary-mismatch medicine sides with Obara's *direction*.** Gluckman & Hanson's "mismatch paradigm" and Lieberman's "mismatch diseases" diagnose obesity, T2 diabetes, myopia, autoimmunity, anxiety, circadian/microbiome disorders as **first-order** body–environment mismatches — mechanistic, not marketing-effects. Had the body fully exteriorised (the limit of Stiegler's tendency), there would be no mismatch diseases; there are, epidemically. → vindicates the irreducible interior, refutes full exteriorisation.
- **But Stiegler's mediated loop has purchase for *diet/compulsion* specifically.** Ultra-processed food *is* sold by a captured attention economy; here "hijacked desire → metabolic disease" is correct. So the evidence is **mixed**: some mismatch is direct (circadian, myopia, sedentariness — Obara's arrow), some is libidinally mediated (junk food, compulsive use — Stiegler's arrow).
- **Self-domestication (Hare 2017; Wrangham 2019; Cieri 2014) refines the interior pole.** The body is *plastic* (reshaped by the artificial — gracilisation, gut–brain trade-off) yet retains a determinate nature that mismatches. So the interior is **active, plastic, persisting-as-determinate**: plasticity in the *form of life* (Stiegler's pole), determinacy in the *substrate's needs* (Obara's pole). Not contradictory.

## 3. The synthesis — both poles, and the loop
Neither thinker gives the whole, because each is one-directional. Holding **two poles** — realist biology (universe) + made meaning (world), squeezed by the **metaverse** — the thesis can theorise the **vicious loop**: the metaverse captures desire (Stiegler) → sells the body precisely what mismatches it — calorie-dense, blue-lit, sedentary, dopamine-timed (Obara's mismatch, now *engineered*) → the dysregulated body craves the captured pleasures the harder → reinforcing. Disindividuation and biological mismatch become **mutually amplifying**; the body's mismatch is increasingly *manufactured* by the same milieu that captures the mind.

Mapped onto the apparatus: the squeeze displaces the **world** (meaning) between the **universe** (the body's evolved needs) and the **metaverse** (engineered artifice); pathology runs on **both axes** because the metaverse drives both. So the thesis **extends Stiegler** (restoring the first-order biological pole he subordinates) *and* **extends Obara** (supplying the libidinal-capture mechanism he underplays) — unifying two half-accounts into one squeeze with two coupled axes. This is the squeeze's pathology, owed to Ch V/VI.
