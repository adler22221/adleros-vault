# tekhne→phusis — FRAMING CLARIFICATION (author, 2026-06-01)

> **Load-bearing. This overrides any literal directional reading of "tekhne is becoming phusis" in the lit-review dossiers (`nomos-phusis-tekhne-CONSOLIDATED`, `nomos-FINDINGS-and-implications`, the PART-A–E files).** Apply in Ch V/VI prose and anywhere the claim appears.

## 1. The claim is NOT literal
"Tekhne is becoming phusis" is a **critical / creative reconstruction and a provocation against the inherited triad phusis / nomos / techne *as they have stood*** — NOT an assertion that the made literally acquires the internal *archē* of the grown. It uses the tradition's own vocabulary to expose the categories' breakdown and to motivate the thesis's replacement apparatus.

## 2. Why — it is anchored in the Ch1 rejection of the nature/culture divide
The thesis **already rejected the nature/culture divide in Chapter 1** [DOCX P0109; Haila 2000; Jones 2009; Obara's duality — "there is no pre-culture nature… the cultural is already what is natural for us"; **"Artificiality – my analytical unit that replaces culture."**]. The inherited **phusis / nomos / techne** map onto the **nature / culture / technology** divides the thesis discards. The author "already knew that going through [the literal phusis/techne] path would be in vain."

## 3. Alignment + divergence (vs Floridi & Latour)
- **Partial alignment:** Floridi (the rich natural/artefactual distinction blurs as the infosphere becomes our world) and Latour (the modern nature/society purification breaks down) reach a kindred rejection of the naive categories — for their own reasons; the thesis reaches it via its ecological/Obara grounding. **Two cautions** (per the Floridi reconstruction): (i) Floridi **explicitly disowns Latour** — so pairing them is the author's **independent-convergence** triangulation, NOT a shared lineage; (ii) Floridi's "marriage of physis/techne" has a **descriptive** side (the blurring) and a **normative** side (the Green/Blue project we must design) — cite only the descriptive side as an ontological claim.
- **Divergence — the thesis's two differentiators:**
  1. It **builds a constructive replacement** — *universe / world / Adaptational Operating System / artificiality* — rather than only deconstructing.
  2. It cuts the realist/constructed line **higher, and names the realist side.** ⚠️ CORRECTION (per the Floridi reconstruction): Floridi is **not** anti-realist and does **not** simply collapse the natural into the artefactual — he keeps a realist **floor** (the noumenal *dedomena* / "constraining affordances," "never itself constructed"; "nothing anti-realist or relativistic"). The real divergence is the **height of the cut**: Floridi puts the realist floor at the bare inferred noumenal *minimum* and lets everything *meaningful* become infosphere/construction; the thesis cuts **higher** and gives the realist side a **thick, named, load-bearing** term — the **universe** ("neither a god nor a man made… unaffectable") — reserving construction for the **world**. So the contrast is one of *level*, not realism-vs-anti-realism. (Full reconstruction: `floridi-argument-reconstruction-2026-06-01.md`. Note also: Floridi's **re-ontologization** — autonomous, out-of-the-loop tech that changes an environment's intrinsic nature — is a near-exact formal analogue of the thesis's **squeeze**, so he is ally on mechanism, rival on the level-of-cut.)
