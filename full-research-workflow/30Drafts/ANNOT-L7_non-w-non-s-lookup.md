## 3. Counter-Evidence — Traditions WITH Significant Domination-Dispositions

### 3.1 What "counter-evidence" means here

These are non-Western non-Sinosphere traditions that DID exhibit significant domination-dispositions — territorial empire, sacrificial cosmology, extractive hierarchy. They are *not* counter-examples to the squeeze hypothesis. They are counter-examples to the *naive* form of the comparative argument that says "the West dominates; the rest do not." The thesis must hold both:

- (a) Domination is widespread across human civilizations.
- (b) The Modern Western *compound* (see §4) is the only one that produced a global technosphere of the contemporary kind.

## 4. Synthesis Point for Ch-3-4 A3

The thesis's Ch-3-4 A3 should NOT claim that "non-Western traditions did not dominate." That claim is empirically false (Aztec, Mongol, Egyptian, Mesopotamian, Ottoman counter-examples) and 馬 or 米 will catch it.

**Recommended formulation:** the disposition to domination is widespread; what is genuinely distinctive about the Modern Western mode is its *combinatorial structure*. The compound has at least five interlocking components, each with non-Western analogues but no other tradition that integrates all five:

1. **Christian universalism** (claim of valid-for-all salvation history) — supplies the imperative to convert the world to one form
2. **Greek metaphysics-of-substance** (subject/object split, naturalism in Descola's sense) — supplies the ontological license to treat nature as standing-reserve
3. **Colonial extractive material base** (post-1492 silver/sugar/labor circuits) — supplies the energetic and capital surplus
4. **Newtonian mechanism** (mathematizable predictable nature) — supplies the operational schema
5. **Capitalist accumulation imperative** (M-C-M' as structural compulsion) — supplies the perpetual self-driving loop

Each component, taken alone, has non-Western analogues. Aztec sacrifice was cosmologically totalizing but lacked Newtonian mechanism. Mongol conquest was extractive but lacked capitalist accumulation logic. Islamic Maragha-school astronomy had mathematical sophistication but was integrated into a contemplative cosmology, not an interventionist one (Hui's cosmotechnics frame; Poskett). Indigenous American polities had hierarchy and at times large-scale state violence (Cahokia, Aztec) but operated within relational ontologies (Descola, Viveiros de Castro) that did not produce the subject/object decomposition.

It is the *systemic technospheric outcome* — the planet-scale infrastructural lock-in that the thesis calls the metaverse-substrate — that no other tradition produced, because no other tradition assembled all five components into a self-reinforcing loop.

This formulation:
- Concedes 馬's comparative-breadth point (domination exists widely)
- Preserves the genealogical specificity of the Modern Western mode (which is the squeeze hypothesis's *causal* claim)
- Makes the argument falsifiable in principle (find a non-Western tradition that assembled all five components and produced a comparable technosphere — there is none)
- Avoids essentialism (the Western compound is itself contingent and historical, not a fixed civilizational essence)

> [VERIFY: this synthesis draws on Hui's cosmotechnics (`CWX5W69K`), Descola's four ontological modes (`6BYZCZ6D`), Pomeranz on Great Divergence (`Y6Z824VX`), Graeber & Wengrow on non-Western political variation (`P:\books(1)` PDF, `[LOOKUP-ZOTERO]`), and Poskett on global science (`489GZ4B9`). Each link is independently verified above; the combinatorial argument itself is the thesis's own.]
