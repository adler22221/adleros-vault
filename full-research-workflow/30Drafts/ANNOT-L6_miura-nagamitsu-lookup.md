## 3. Most Relevant Work for Ch-3-4 A5

**Primary candidate: `miuraKankyoShisoTo2006` — 環境思想と社会：思想史的アプローチ (2006)**

This is the strongest fit for Ch-3-4 A5 because:
- The subtitle **"思想史的アプローチ"** (a thought-historical approach) signals exactly what A5 needs: a Japanese philosopher working *through* the Western philosophical genealogy rather than around it.
- A book-length treatment (御茶の水書房 is a respected academic press) gives breadth across multiple Western thinkers, allowing the thesis to cite a single non-Eurocentric voice that already does the comparative work — rather than juxtaposing isolated quotes.
- Already tagged `nlm:synced`, so verification via Notebook A-Sources is available without further upload.
- 環境思想 (kankyō shisō / environmental thought) intersects directly with the universe/world/metaverse "squeeze" framing: the displacement of the human by both natural-substrate constraints and constructed meaning-spaces is precisely the terrain Miura works.
