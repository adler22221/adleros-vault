# Gemini/Imagen Illustration Prompts — Five Dependency Modes

Conceptual illustrations for the JASA 2026 conference slide deck. One illustration per dependency mode (天地依存 / 配分依存 / 貨幣依存 / 人工性依存 / 制御性依存). Designed to sit in the right ~40% of a 16:9 slide, with Japanese labels added afterward in PowerPoint.

## How to use

1. Paste the **Shared Style Preamble** below at the start of every one of the five prompts (or as a persistent system/style instruction if your tool supports it).
2. **Generate Image 1 (天地依存) first.** Once you're happy with it, feed it back in as a style/reference image ("match the style, palette, and rendering of this reference image") for Images 2–5. This keeps line weight, shading, and palette consistent across the set of five, since each mode still needs its own dominant color and distinct metaphor.
3. Suggested aspect ratio: **4:3** (or 1:1 square) — easiest to place and crop into the right-hand 40% of a 16:9 slide without awkward empty margins. Avoid generating in native 16:9, as the single centered focal metaphor will end up too small.
4. If the model insists on adding text/labels/watermarks, add to the prompt: "absolutely no text, no typography, no numerals, no UI elements of any kind."

## Shared Style Preamble (paste before every prompt)

> Style: flat, minimal, editorial vector-style illustration for a serious academic conference slide deck — NOT watercolor, NOT painterly, NOT textured, NOT 3D, NOT glossy, NOT photorealistic. Clean geometric-leaning shapes that go beyond pure geometry into simple stylized human figures and objects (depictive and metaphorical, not abstract-only). Matte solid color fills only, no gradients, no noise/grain, at most a soft flat drop shadow (no heavy or glossy shadows). Absolutely no text, no letters, no numbers, no typography, no UI elements anywhere in the image. Background: transparent or plain white. Composition: single clear focal metaphor, centered, generous negative space around it, suitable for cropping into the right ~40% of a 16:9 slide. Locked color palette across the full set of five illustrations (use only these colors plus white): deep pine green #2E6E5A, slate blue #41678A, warm amber #C98A2E, deep navy #16233B, muted gray #6B7280. Each individual illustration should be dominated by its own assigned mode color, with the other palette colors appearing only as small accents, so all five images read as one cohesive set. Style anchors: flat editorial vector illustration, muted academic palette, subtle grain-free flat shading, restrained, conceptual, dignified — think a serious systems-theory or philosophy talk, not a children's book or startup pitch deck.

## Prompt 1 — 天地依存 (Heaven-Earth dependence) — dominant color: deep pine green #2E6E5A

Flat editorial vector illustration, dominant color deep pine green #2E6E5A, following the shared style preamble above. Depict a gentle circular composition representing the ecological cycle of hunting-gathering, farming, and nomadic herding: three or four small, simple stylized human figures rendered in flat silhouette are arranged around a soft ring, each directly engaged with the living earth — one bends to till soil, one gathers from a stylized plant or bush, one tends a simple grazing animal (a flat-shape sheep or goat), one reaches up toward a stylized sun and falling raindrops. Connect the figures and elements with a thin, calm circular arrow or flow-line suggesting a closed metabolic loop between human, soil, rain, sun, and animal — no beginning or end, only continuous exchange. Use warm earth-toned accents (small touches of the amber #C98A2E) for sun and grain against the dominant pine green for land, plants, and figures, with slate blue #41678A used sparingly for water/rain. Keep every shape flat, matte, and simple — no fine texture on soil or foliage, just clean flat silhouettes suggesting land, water, sun, and living things in quiet reciprocity. Transparent or plain white background, centered composition, generous negative space, no text anywhere.

## Prompt 2 — 配分依存 (Allocation dependence) — dominant color: slate blue #41678A

Flat editorial vector illustration, dominant color slate blue #41678A, following the shared style preamble above. Center the image on a single simple stylized human figure actively shaving or trimming a piece of itself away with a small flat tool, so its outline conforms to a rigid pre-set geometric frame or slot (a stark rectangular or keyhole-shaped mold outlined in deep navy #16233B) that it is standing inside — visually show a thin sliver of the figure's silhouette being pared off to fit the mold's edge. To the side, depict a gradient of two to three smaller stylized figures at varying distances from a matching mold, each receiving a flow of small flat token/block shapes (representing resources) whose size and quantity scale directly with how closely each figure's silhouette matches its frame: the well-fitted figure receives a dense cluster of larger tokens, the poorly-fitted figure receives only one or two small, thin tokens. Render the resource tokens in warm amber #C98A2E as a small accent color against the dominant slate blue figures and frames. Keep all shapes flat and matte with no gradients, clean edges, plain white or transparent background, centered composition with clear negative space, no text or numerals anywhere.

## Prompt 3 — 貨幣依存 (Money dependence) — dominant color: warm amber #C98A2E

Flat editorial vector illustration, dominant color warm amber #C98A2E, following the shared style preamble above. Compose a closed circular chain of four to six small stylized human figures arranged in a ring, each one reaching a flat, simple hand or arm toward the next figure's chest or open vessel — depict each figure holding or standing beside a small flat empty cup, bowl, or hollow container shape that is never filled, symbolizing perpetual, self-sustaining lack. The reaching hands and empty vessels form a continuous closed loop with a faint circular arrow suggesting endless motion without resolution — no figure's vessel is ever topped up, and each figure's outstretched reach simultaneously keeps the next figure wanting. Render the figures and vessels in dominant amber tones with the circular connecting loop in a slightly darker amber or muted gray #6B7280 accent, and only a sparing touch of deep navy #16233B for shadow or outline definition. Flat matte fills only, no gradients or texture, transparent or plain white background, centered composition with the ring taking up a moderate, balanced portion of the frame surrounded by negative space, no text or numerals anywhere.

## Prompt 4 — 人工性依存 (Artificiality dependence) — dominant color: muted gray #6B7280

Flat editorial vector illustration, dominant color muted gray #6B7280 with small color accents, following the shared style preamble above. Center a single simple stylized human figure (rendered in flat muted gray, arms slightly open) surrounded by four small paired icons, each pairing a pre-modern tool with the body part it externalizes, arranged around the figure like satellites: (1) a closed flat book shape positioned near the figure's head, paired subtly with a faint brain-outline motif; (2) a few flat stylized written characters or brush-stroke marks positioned near the figure's mouth, paired with a faint mouth/speech motif — render these as abstract calligraphic marks, not legible text; (3) a simple flat horse-drawn carriage or cart-with-wheel shape positioned near the figure's legs and feet; (4) a flat two-wheeled handcart shape positioned near the figure's hands. Each tool-icon should visually and compositionally "extend" from its corresponding body part with a thin connecting line, like a diagram of externalized organs, all rendered in the dominant muted gray with only small warm accent touches (a hint of amber #C98A2E or pine green #2E6E5A) on one or two of the tool icons to keep them legible against the gray figure. Keep everything strictly pre-modern and hand/animal-powered — no wheels-as-engines, no electricity, no digital or modern machinery of any kind. Flat matte shapes, transparent or plain white background, centered composition, generous negative space, no text, no letters, no numerals anywhere.

## Prompt 5 — 制御性依存 (Controllability dependence) — dominant color: deep navy #16233B

Flat editorial vector illustration, dominant color deep navy #16233B, following the shared style preamble above. Depict a single simple stylized human figure standing in a narrow vertical gap at the exact center of the composition, visibly compressed with shoulders drawn slightly inward, caught between two converging forces pressing in from either side. On the left, render a collapsing, destabilized natural landscape — cracked earth, a wilting or broken tree silhouette, and jagged unstable ground shapes in deep pine green #2E6E5A — tilting inward toward the figure like an encroaching wall of failing nature (the universe). On the right, render an angular artificial lattice or grid structure made of thin flat geometric nodes and connecting lines suggestive of an AI-and-IoT-built system (a metaverse-like mesh), rendered in slate blue #41678A, also tilting inward toward the figure from the opposite side, its grid lines narrowing like a closing structure. Both converging forces should visually taper toward the human figure at the center, leaving it in an ever-narrowing vertical gap of plain white negative space, dominant deep navy used for the figure itself and for the darkest structural elements of both pressing forces to unify the palette. Flat matte fills, no gradients or texture, transparent or plain white background, centered composition emphasizing the squeeze, no text, no letters, no numerals anywhere.
