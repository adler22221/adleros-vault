> "the virtual is no longer an epistemic construction that exists exclusively for the benefit of human beings. Virtual reality is not merely populated by objects, states, events, and actions that serve human purposes. Machines adapt the representation of the world to their own functioning and build up a virtual reality that provides the most suitable environment for their operations." (p. 320)

**Author's annotation [^2]:** "Supports my view that the [[metaverse]] is a different kind of environment/domain, analogous to but distinct from the [[universe]], which imposes evolutionary/adaptational pressure to its member agents."
