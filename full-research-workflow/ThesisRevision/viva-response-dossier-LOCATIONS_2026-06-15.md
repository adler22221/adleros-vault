# How the Final Thesis Addresses the Committee's Comments
**Thesis:** *Between the Universe and the Metaverse: Surviving the Squeeze in Between* · **Author:** Adler Yang (楊逸帆)
**Committee:** Kai Marchal (馬愷之) · Chienkuo Mi (米建國, supervisor) · Yucheng Liu (劉育成) · **Dept of Philosophy, Soochow University** · **2026-06-15**

Anchors match the TOC headings exactly. Committee comments are reconstructed from brief viva notes — the phrasing interprets each concern.

**At a glance — the expansion *is* the response.** The three voices converged: **Mi** "too short / too unclear"; **Liu** "論證不足 — enough for an MA?"; **Marchal** "make the squeeze the core and explain it." The revision answers with a **~7.5× expansion** (≈10,000 → ~75,000 words; 8 developed chapters; **169 references, 83 footnotes, 9 tables**) that turns every stub into an argued chapter and makes the squeeze the spine.
