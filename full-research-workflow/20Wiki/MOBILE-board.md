## 🛠 If LIVE mode breaks

**Symptoms you'd see:** Claude mobile app shows "thesis-revision-mobile" as offline (no green dot), or chat doesn't respond.

**Recovery in order:**
1. Wait 30 sec, pull-to-refresh the Code-tab session list
2. After 1 min, reconnect by name (the session id may have rotated after a wrapper restart)
3. After 3 min, **edit [MOBILE-TROUBLESHOOT.md](../MOBILE-TROUBLESHOOT.md)** with what you see → response appears in [MOBILE-TROUBLESHOOT-RESPONSE.md](../MOBILE-TROUBLESHOOT-RESPONSE.md) within ~2 min
4. After 5 min with no troubleshoot response, the watcher itself may be down → **switch to FALLBACK** by creating `MOBILE-FALLBACK.txt` at project root

In FALLBACK mode: your dictations into MOBILE-prompts.md and MOBILE-dictation.md still sync to PC and will be integrated when LIVE / a fresh PC session resumes.
