## §1 — The squeeze defined (the constitutive thesis)

The universe-metaverse squeeze is **the condition of a being that must simultaneously satisfy the selection criteria of two environments whose organizing laws are not mutually translatable**.

Two features constitute the squeeze as such:

### F1. Two-environment between-structure (constitutive at two subject-levels)

The subject is *between* two environments — not within one environment subject to new pressures. Each environment has its own organizing principles (real-level generative structures, per F2) under which the subject's adaptation / success is jointly constrained. No single optimization is possible across the two.

**The subject-level is dual** (per §1.3):
- **Level 1 — Organism.** An individual member of a species (human or other creature) whose adaptive capacities are jointly constrained by two environments at the organism-as-adaptive-unit level.
- **Level 2 — World-as-OS.** An anthropological human OS, constituted by a configuration of means (institutions, technologies, practices, infrastructures, social arrangements), whose structural integrity is jointly constrained by two background environments at the world-as-structural-OS level.

F1 is satisfied at either level independently, and the two levels are mechanistically coupled (via 自己完結化 ↔ 自足完成化; see §1.3 "Two-level squeeze"). The lock's F1 claim is therefore not just "an organism between two environments" but the more general structural condition under which both individual organisms AND worlds-as-systems-of-means stand between incommensurable real-level environments.

**The "between" structure is not new at the metaverse-stage.** Prior squeezes (animal F1+F2 squeezes since late Pleistocene; human F1+F2 squeezes since Obara's self-domestication onset) also satisfied F1 — they were between universe-originated ecosystems and human-created artificial ecosystems at the dominantly conventional register. The metaverse-stage's distinctness lies in F2's register-transgression and §1.3's stratified two-environment configuration (where the artificial side constitutes a *real-level background environment* of its own), not in F1's between-structure as such.

### F2. Incommensurability of organizing principles (at the real level)

The organizing principles of the two environments — the *generative structures at the real level* that determine which adaptational regimes are operative — are not mutually translatable. F2 is a claim about the real-level laws, not about empirical-level signatures of how the environments are experienced.

- **The universe** operates through transfactual laws (thermodynamic, biological, ecological, geographic, chemical) whose generative-causal structures are naturally given and operate independently of human enactment.
- **The metaverse**, at the metaverse-stage (per §1.3 Aspect A), operates through engineered laws — Yang's 自足完成化 at the meta-law stratum plus the architectural-mechanism stratum (Lessig's architecture-as-regulation; Hui's productive-ontologies-via-associated-milieus; the recursive coupling of tertiary retention and tertiary protention; see §V §1–§2). These are humanly produced (nomos-origin) but transfactually compelling once instantiated (physis-operation).
- **Pre-metaverse human-created artificial ecosystems** operate through their own real-level structures (institutions, supply chains, infrastructural systems, agricultural practices, market structures) at the dominantly conventional register. The F2 incommensurability between these and universe-laws is what constitutes the pre-metaverse human and animal F1+F2 squeezes.

F2 says: the generative structures of these environments are not mutually translatable. They are not different points on a common scale; they have no common measure that would allow a single trade-off calculation. This is incommensurability in the strict sense (after Kuhn / Feyerabend, but applied to *environments-as-generative-structures* rather than to scientific paradigms).

**What F2 does NOT directly assert** (to avoid Bhaskar-level conflation):
- F2 is not the empirical-level claim that *competencies developed in one are non-transferable to the other*, although that empirical observation is downstream of F2
- F2 is not the empirical-level claim that *value-currencies are non-exchangeable*, although exchange-failures observed across environments are downstream of F2
- F2 is not the question of *which selection signatures* (embodied resilience vs. platform legibility, etc.) each environment promotes — those are empirical-level operational signatures whose proper home is V-3 v3 §4 (metaverse-side: opacity, mutability, structural compulsion, extraction, optimization, temporal acceleration) and §VI.4 (the universe-side / metaverse-side selection-criteria contrast at the lived-experience level)
- F2 is not about *which-laws-apply* (gravity still applies in metaverse-mediated environments; what is incommensurable is which generative structures determine fitness / structural integrity in each environment)

F2 stays at the real level: the laws are incommensurable as *generative structures*, prior to any empirical observation of the consequences.

### §1.3 — Structural form, species-and-OS relativity, and the metaverse-stage condition

F1+F2 specify a structural condition that is **neither species-specific nor specific to the metaverse-stage**. The condition obtains whenever a subject (organism, or world-as-OS) is jointly constrained by two environments whose organizing principles (selection criteria, fitness measures, niche-organizing logics) are not mutually translatable. Both pre-metaverse cases and the metaverse-stage are instances of F1+F2; what differs is the specific filling of the two environments, the register of the artificial-side organizing principles, and — at the metaverse-stage — the stratified two-environment configuration described below.

**Ideal-type usage.** Throughout this revision and the lock generally, "world" and "mode of artificiality" are used as Weberian heuristic-comparative constructs (ideal types) — not Platonic forms, not normative ideals, not Husserlian essences. Each world (as ideal type) is built upon its own ideal-typical mode of artificiality (the artifice-making subset of its collective survival / adaptational strategies). This usage is heuristic and analytical; it commits to no essentialist or normative reading.

#### What organisms experience

Organisms — humans and other creatures alike — experience both universe-laws directly (gravity acting on the falling body; thermodynamics on the thermoregulating body; chemical-biological laws on metabolism) AND universe-law manifestations (local ecosystems, climate, terrain, food web, pathogen spectrum, the historical-geographic conditions that produce the lived environment). Both layers are operative; the F2 incommensurability is at the level of organizing principles / selection criteria, not at the level of which-laws-apply.

#### Always-existing squeeze (humans + other creatures, since the relevant niche-construction transitions)

The F2 incommensurability between *universe-originated ecosystems* and *human-created artificial ecosystems* applies continuously:

- **Other-creatures case**: universe-originated niche manifestations (predator behavior, plant chemistry, climate, native pathogens, landscape connectivity, reproductive phenology) ⇄ human-created artificial-ecosystem manifestations (human proximity, crop/refuse food sources, traps and firearms, roads and fences, light/noise/chemical pollution, climate-altered conditions). The structural form has been the operative condition for many non-human species for at least fifty thousand years; the empirical record is the record of anthropogenic extinction (cf. §VI.2), now situated within the broader Anthropocene literature (Crutzen + Stoermer 2000; Steffen et al. 2007; Chakrabarty 2009; Zalasiewicz et al.; Haraway 2015; Moore on Capitalocene; Tsing 2015 on multispecies).

- **Human case (pre-metaverse and ongoing)**: universe-originated planetary manifestations ⇄ human-created artificial-niche manifestations. The intensification of this squeeze across human history is captured by Obara's trajectory from 自己家畜化 (self-domestication — humans adapting by constructing self-made habitats) to 自己ペット化 (self-petification — humans becoming "pets" of their own systems via 社会のカプセル化 / 相互ドレイ制), with Uegaki's 自己完結化 (the human-side tendency toward life-as-willed via delegation to artificiality) as the system-side closure logic. These dynamics constitute the human pre-metaverse F1+F2 squeeze; the metaverse-stage inherits, rather than initiates, it.

Pre-metaverse human-created artificial ecosystems are NOT "merely manifestations under universe-laws." They have their own real-level generative structures (institutions, supply chains, infrastructural systems, agricultural practices, market structures), with a **dominantly conventional register**: those structures are practice-enacted, contestable, and persist through ongoing human performance, with structural margins (legal coercion enforced by state monopoly on violence; infrastructural lock-in; market compulsion) that do not dominate the register.

#### The new condition at the metaverse-stage — the stratified two-environment configuration

What is structurally new at the metaverse-stage is the emergence of a *second real-level background environment* (the metaverse) parallel to but incommensurable with the universe. The metaverse-laws — the real-level stratum of the metaverse — comprise the meta-law of self-completion (Yang's 自足完成化, extending Uegaki's 自己完結化: the disposition of artificiality to become self-sufficient, capable of operating and maintaining itself without ongoing human direction) and the architectural-mechanism claims developed in §V (Lessig's architecture-as-regulation; Hui's productive-ontologies-via-associated-milieus; the recursive coupling of tertiary retention and tertiary protention).

The metaverse-stage condition is constituted by two structurally-inseparable aspects:

**Aspect A — Metaverse-laws as a new term.** A new kind of law enters the system: **conventional in origin** (made / decided / created / engineered by humans — nomos register) **AND structural in operation** `[TERMINOLOGY-PENDING]` (compelling, transfactual, self-completing without ongoing human enactment — physis-operation register). This transgresses the classical νόμος / φύσις pair: nomos-origin + physis-operation in a single structural fact that the classical pair could not anticipate.

> **Defensive note on "structural"** `[TERMINOLOGY-PENDING]` *— working signpost, 2026-05-14*. By "structural" we mean: **compliance secured by the structure of the situation** — the architecture, affordances, and operational dynamics of the artificiality — rather than by social agreement. The contrast with "conventional" is *modal*: a conventional rule remains in force when violated (it is contestable in principle, even when enforced through sanction); a structural rule **forecloses the violation as an action-possibility within the field**. The structural register is not Humean constant-conjunction causality, and it is not merely the generic critical-realist claim that real-level structures have causal powers — every real-level structure does. What "structural" names here is the specific case in which an artificiality's architecture / affordance-field structurally circumscribes the action-possibilities of those embedded in it (Gibson affordances / Lessig architecture-as-regulation / Foucault dispositif / Hui productive-ontologies-via-associated-milieus). The term accommodates both deterministic mechanisms (a locked door; gravity) and probabilistic mechanisms (LLM next-token distributions; recommender-system rankings): in both cases compliance is enforced by the structure of the situation rather than by ongoing social enactment. "Structural" applies symmetrically to cell 1 (physical-structural: universe-laws operating on substrate) and cell 4 (engineered-structural: algorithmic architecture, platform affordances). Earlier placeholder "causally-binding" was rejected (Humean / generic-CR misread risk; doesn't name structural foreclosure of action-possibility); "necessitarian" was rejected before that (metaphysical-modal burden). Term to be re-evaluated at final-draft pass; "structurally compelling" is the leading alternative if "structural" reads too thin.

**As 自足完成化 progresses, the conventional space — for decision, revision, contestation — progressively shrinks.** Initial decisions become harder to revise once the system matures; algorithmic systems become entrenched; mutations become path-dependent on prior architectural choices; auditability decreases. Metaverse-laws thus begin in the conventional register (decided, designed, written) but migrate toward an increasingly closed structural register where the conventional revision-space disappears.

**Aspect B — What metaverse-laws DO to the universe-side terms.** Metaverse-laws act on the field of universe-law manifestations that organisms inhabit. The specific operations include [terms pending author endorsement; Claude's working placeholders are "mediation" (navigation apps standing between humans and spatial experience; algorithmic agriculture standing between livestock and feeding regimes; satellite tracking standing between wildlife migration and data), "overlay" (data streams laid on top of physical environments; sensor networks over ecosystems; AI-mediated conservation management over wildlife habitats), and "hybridization" (smart cities reshaping urban wildlife; algorithmic agriculture transforming livestock conditions; environmental sensing redefining wildlife observation; platform-mediated human social environments)]. These operations are the metaverse-stage extension of the Anthropocene's structural agency: pre-metaverse Anthropocene Aspect-B-analogue (climate change, biosphere alteration, ocean chemistry shifts, geological-stratigraphic effects) operated through practice-enacted means in the dominantly conventional register; metaverse-stage Aspect B extends this through self-completing means in the structural-operation register, intensifying and reorganizing the Anthropocene's effects on universe-law manifestations.

Aspects A and B are inseparable: A names what metaverse-laws *are* (a new kind of law); B names what they *do* (act on universe-law manifestations). Without B, A is merely a parallel laws-system; without A, B is merely intensified manifestation-level competition. The structural novelty is constituted by both together.

#### Two-level squeeze

Under the metaverse-stage condition, F1+F2 applies at two structurally distinct but mechanistically coupled levels:

- **Level 1 — Organisms-as-individuals squeezed.** Individual humans (acutely) and other creatures (increasingly, via Aspect B) face incommensurable selection criteria from the universe-originated and artificial-side environments. For humans, this is the Obara-style petification squeeze whose psychosomatic / existential / livelihood-precarity manifestations §VI(A) develops. For other creatures, this is the Anthropocene's structural agency on wildlife, livestock, marine life, and ecosystems generally, now extended through metaverse-stage Aspect B (algorithmic agriculture, smart-city infrastructure, environmental sensing, satellite tracking, AI-mediated conservation).

- **Level 2 — Worlds-as-systems-of-means squeezed.** The *world itself* — as an OS-specific-to-anthropological-humans constituted by a configuration of *means* (institutions, technologies, practices, infrastructures, social arrangements) — is now also squeezed between universe and metaverse. The world diminishes as its components migrate from "internal means" to "background environment": pre-metaverse the means are internal to the world and serve human purposes; through the bidirectional coupling of 自己完結化 (Uegaki, human-side delegation) and 自足完成化 (Yang, apparatus-side self-completion), the means progressively gain operational independence; once they self-complete sufficiently they cease to be internal means and constitute a *background environment of their own* — the metaverse. The world's own components leave the world and become its pressure. The world contracts; the 役割 humans play in the world shrinks (cf. §V §1.2). The world is now squeezed between the universe (always-prior background) and the metaverse (newly-emergent background, constituted by what was previously the world's own means).

Level 1 and Level 2 are mechanistically coupled. The individual-level squeeze (Level 1) drives the world-diminishing dynamic (Level 2) via 自己完結化: as individuals delegate to means, the means become independent, the world contracts. Conversely, as the world contracts, individuals find themselves in a thinner world whose means have migrated to its background, intensifying their Level-1 squeeze. The coupling is bidirectional and self-reinforcing, structurally identical to the 自己完結化 ↔ 自足完成化 bidirectional coupling at the structural-OS level.

F1 (between-structure) thus applies at Level 1 (organisms between universe and metaverse manifestation fields) AND at Level 2 (worlds between universe and metaverse as background environments). F2 (incommensurability) applies at both levels: incommensurable selection criteria for organisms; incommensurable structural pressures for worlds.

#### Environmentalist-stratified, not organicist-fused

The metaverse-laws constitute a new *real-level background environment with its own laws*, NOT a Hui-style organizing-inorganic superorganism that fuses apparatus and environment. The thesis keeps both poles real: the universe remains a real-level background environment with its own transfactual laws indifferent to data-generativity; the metaverse becomes a *new* real-level background environment with its self-completing tendency selecting for data-generativity. Humans (and other creatures via Aspect B) are caught not inside a single superorganism but between two incommensurable real-level environments. This commitment is methodologically critical: the stratified two-environment configuration is articulable only if both poles remain real.

#### The §III-8 / §VI boundary

§III-8 establishes that (i) the modern Western mode of artificiality is one of many modes; (ii) modes are *collective strategies of adaptation / survival*; (iii) modes can fail when pressured by the universe or its planetary manifestations; (iv) this applies to both human worlds AND animal operating systems. The F1+F2 lock developed here specifies the *structural form* of the squeeze that, in its metaverse-stage stratified-two-environment-configuration instantiation, characterizes why the modern Western mode at the metaverse-stage represents a structurally novel vulnerability — the conventionally-originated-but-structural-in-operation register with progressively shrinking conventional space, plus Aspect B's reorganization of the universe-side manifestation field. §III-8 ↔ §VI thus operate complementarily: §III-8 establishes modal pluralism + the universal mode-failure-under-pressure point; §VI specifies the structural-form analysis of the metaverse-stage's unique configuration. Neither replaces the other; the earlier framing that treated §III-8 as a "single-environment failure" foil to §VI's "two-environment squeeze" is dropped.

## §1b — Squeeze versus collapse: an analytical distinction

The squeeze and collapse are distinct phenomena that must not be conflated.

The squeeze is the *ongoing structural condition* of being jointly subject to incommensurable organizing principles / selection criteria (F1+F2 above), operative at the individual-organism level (Level 1) and at the world-as-system-of-means level (Level 2) — see §1.3. It is a condition that obtains for as long as F1+F2 hold at either or both levels. The squeeze can be lived with — for finite intervals, under specific conditions of recognition and navigation (cf. §3 productive dimension, dependency-turn).

Collapse is what happens at the endpoint when the squeeze becomes irreversible and the squeezed being can no longer sustain dual satisfaction. Collapse is the *crossing of a threshold* beyond which the structural strain breaks the subject's capacity to operate at all. For an organism, collapse means extinction; for a civilization or world (Level 2), collapse means the loss of sociopolitical complexity or the dissolution of the world-as-OS into its now-independent components; for a person, collapse means the breakdown of the dual operation by which one was navigating both environments.

Prior squeezes (animal F1+F2 squeezes since late Pleistocene; human F1+F2 squeezes since Obara's self-domestication onset, intensifying through self-petification) operated at the dominantly conventional register of human-created artificial-ecosystem organizing principles. Prior collapses (civilizational examples — Roman, Maya, Easter Island; ongoing animal extinctions) are collapse-endpoints of these conventional-register squeezes. The metaverse-stage squeeze is distinguished from prior squeezes not by being the first F1+F2 squeeze (it is not) but by (i) the stratified two-environment configuration, in which metaverse-laws constitute a new real-level background environment parallel to the universe; (ii) the conventional-origin + structural-operation register-transgression with progressively shrinking conventional space (per §1.3); and (iii) the two-level squeeze in which worlds-as-systems-of-means are also F1+F2 subjects.

The analytical resources for squeeze and collapse come from different literatures. The squeeze is analyzed through structural-environmental concepts (F1+F2 above; the operational features F3–F5; the bidirectional coupling of 自己完結化 ↔ 自足完成化 specified in §V §1.2; the stratified two-environment configuration in §1.3). Collapse is analyzed through different literatures — Tainter's complexity-maintenance equations, Holling's adaptive cycle, Perrow's normal accidents, Homer-Dixon's tight-coupling synthesis, Taleb's fragility/antifragility, Schwartz and Nichols's regeneration-base claim — each of which addresses the threshold-crossing rather than the structural condition that precedes it.

The distinction matters for two reasons. First, the diagnostic urgency of the squeeze is misread if it is conflated with the diagnostic urgency of collapse. The squeeze can be structurally severe without producing immediate collapse for finite intervals; the squeeze can also approach its collapse endpoint without warning. The distinction is necessary to maintain analytical precision about which claim is being made.

Second, the *thesis's distinctive contribution at the collapse-prospect stratum* — Claim C: the metaverse-stage destroys its own regeneration-base — is structurally distinct from the squeeze claim. The squeeze claim is structural-ontological (F1+F2 at two levels under the stratified two-environment configuration). Claim C is a structural-prognosis at the collapse-endpoint stratum, integrating the V-5 convergent-diagnosis material with Schwartz and Nichols's regeneration-base concept. Keeping squeeze and collapse analytically separate is what allows the thesis to deliver both claims without conflation.

## §2 — How this squeeze differs from prior squeezes (the differentiating thesis)

This addresses Liu's question directly: 那個squeeze跟過去的有何不同？

> **Note (2026-05-12 integration):** The earlier framing of this section treated prior squeezes as "within-world or within-universe transformations" that did not satisfy F1+F2. Per Revision 3 (§1.3) and Revision 2 (§1b), this framing is now superseded. Prior squeezes — both animal F1+F2 squeezes since the late Pleistocene and human F1+F2 squeezes since Obara's self-domestication onset — DID satisfy F1+F2 (between-environment, incommensurable organizing principles), but they operated at the **dominantly conventional register** of human-created artificial-ecosystem organizing principles. The metaverse-stage squeeze is distinguished not by being the *first* F1+F2 squeeze but by its **stratified two-environment configuration** (§1.3) in which metaverse-laws constitute a new real-level background environment parallel to the universe, with conventional-origin + structural-operation register-transgression and the two-level squeeze (Level 1 organisms + Level 2 worlds-diminishing).

Prior technological transitions imposed real adaptational pressures (foraging→agriculture, craft→industry, rural→urban, analog→digital communication). These transitions intensified the long-standing human F1+F2 squeeze (between universe-originated ecosystems and human-created artificial ecosystems, per Obara's self-domestication → self-petification trajectory) at the dominantly conventional register. The factory, the railroad, the city, the radio: each elaborated the conventional-register human-created artificial-ecosystem without (yet) constituting a parallel real-level background environment with its own engineered laws.

The metaverse-stage squeeze is categorically distinct because it satisfies F1+F2 *under the stratified two-environment configuration* (§1.3) — it is genuinely between two real-level background environments rather than between universe-originated and conventional-register human-created artificial ecosystems alone. The metaverse-laws are engineered, transfactually-operating, self-completing, with progressively shrinking conventional revision-space.

This categorical distinction manifests through three operational features. F3 has been reframed (per Revision 1) to describe the stratified two-environment dynamism rather than a simple law-change asymmetry; F4 and F5 retain their pre-revision formulations but are read in light of §1.3:

### F3. Stratified two-environment dynamism with shrinking conventional space

The metaverse-stage stratified two-environment configuration (§1.3) produces a distinctive dynamism pattern across its constituent terms. The configuration is not a binary squeeze with one stable and one unstable pole; it is a configuration of universe-laws, universe-law manifestations, and metaverse-laws each operating with distinct dynamism profiles.

**Universe-laws: transfactually stable.** The laws of the universe — thermodynamic, biological, ecological, geographic, chemical — are stable on the timescale of human civilization at the real level of Bhaskar's stratification: causally-generative structures that hold whether or not they are exercised in any specific event. Universe-laws are not what the metaverse-stage subject contends with as a moving target. Organisms — humans and other creatures — experience these laws *directly* (gravity acting on the falling body; thermodynamics on the thermoregulating body; chemical-biological laws on metabolism) AND through their manifestations. The F2 incommensurability is at the level of organizing principles, not at the level of which-laws-apply: universe-laws still apply in metaverse-mediated environments; what's incommensurable is which selection criteria are operative.

**Universe-law manifestations: dynamic under anthropogenic disturbance.** The planetary manifestations of universe-laws — at the actual level — are dynamic on civilizational timescales, increasingly produced by human worlds. Climate is changing because of anthropogenic emissions; biosphere composition is being altered through habitat conversion and species translocation; ocean chemistry is shifting through carbon dioxide absorption; atmospheric composition is being engineered through fossil-fuel combustion. The Anthropocene literature (Crutzen + Stoermer 2000; Steffen et al. 2007; Chakrabarty 2009; Zalasiewicz et al.; Haraway 2015; Moore on Capitalocene; Tsing 2015) documents this dynamism. At the metaverse-stage, Aspect B (§1.3) extends this Anthropocene agency through new self-completing means: algorithmic agriculture, IoT environmental monitoring, AI-mediated conservation, smart cities, satellite tracking, drone-based ecology, computational ocean management. The universe-side manifestation field is increasingly metaverse-mediated.

**Metaverse-laws: conventional in origin, structural in operation, with conventional space progressively shrinking.** The laws of the metaverse — 自足完成化 at the meta-law stratum (§V §1); architectural-mechanism claims (Lessig's architecture-as-regulation; Hui's productive-ontologies-via-associated-milieus; recursive retention/protention coupling; §V §2); operationalized through the four minimally sufficient technologies (AI / big data / IoT / code-as-law; §V §3) — are deliberately engineered (conventional in origin: humanly produced, made / decided / created), revisable in principle, often deliberately obscured. They operate in the **structural register** `[TERMINOLOGY-PENDING]` (see defensive note in §1.3 Aspect A): once instantiated, they compel transfactually through their architecture / affordance-field, regardless of ongoing human enactment, accommodating both deterministic mechanisms and probabilistic ML. As 自足完成化 progresses, the conventional space — for decision, revision, contestation — progressively shrinks: initial decisions become harder to revise once the system reaches operational maturity; algorithmic systems become entrenched; mutations become path-dependent on prior architectural choices; auditability decreases. Metaverse-laws thus migrate from a dominantly conventional opening to an increasingly closed structural register, transgressing the classical νόμος / φύσις pair: nomos-origin + physis-operation in a single structural fact that the classical pair could not anticipate.

**The configuration-wide dynamism.** Each of the three terms moves with its own dynamism profile: universe-laws stable; universe-law manifestations dynamic under anthropogenic and metaverse-extended disturbance; metaverse-laws engineered, with revision-space progressively closing. The metaverse-stage subject — humans acutely, other creatures via Aspect B — adapts to a configuration whose components are moving for structurally different reasons, none of which is the stable fallback substrate that prior squeezes could presume to inhabit.

Prior F1+F2 squeezes (the pre-metaverse Obara-trajectory squeezes for humans; the late-Pleistocene-onward squeezes for many other species) operated against the relatively stable background of universe-law manifestations and a dominantly conventional artificial-side. The metaverse-stage configuration adds (i) dynamism at the universe-law manifestation level under anthropogenic and metaverse-extended disturbance, and (ii) the engineered, progressively-closing artificial-side organizing principles. The double-source of instability is the operational feature of the metaverse-stage F3.

### F4. Substrate-decoupling

Prior technologies operated on the universe-substrate — the steam engine, the telegraph, the assembly line all altered material conditions while remaining tethered to thermodynamics, geography, and biology. The metaverse's organizing logic is designed to be **substrate-independent**: geographically placeless, somatically disembedded, indifferent to embodied presence. This decoupling is what makes it a genuinely new environment rather than a transformed feature of the universe.

(Note: substrate-independence is *aspirational and partial*, not absolute — the metaverse still requires servers, electricity, rare earths, embodied users. The point is that its organizing logic does not regulate or acknowledge these dependencies. This itself produces the squeeze: the universe-substrate continues to make demands the metaverse refuses to register.)

### F5. Radical monopoly over livelihood

(After Illich, *Tools for Conviviality*, 1973.) Prior technological transitions created new dependencies but left alternative universe-anchored pathways viable in principle. Rural livelihood remained possible after urbanization; craft remained possible after industrialization. The metaverse's radical monopoly tends toward the **elimination of all non-platform-mediated pathways** — not through prohibition but through the restructuring of the conditions under which any livelihood can succeed (banking, identity verification, civic participation, professional credentialing, social belonging, all increasingly platform-gated). This makes the present squeeze uniquely total: it offers no refuge even in principle.

## §3 — Productive dimension of the squeeze (the qualification)

> **Derivation tier:** [T1: source-ms-kaikan §2.4, §6] for structural argument and 依存転回 framing; **[T0: NLM-Uegaki-A-Sources, conv 15df9238]** for 〈意のままになる生〉 normative characterization, structural costs, and ecological/evolutionary framing. See `.research/uegaki-tier0-retrieval.md` for full retrieved passages.

This addresses Ma's and Liu's pushback that the squeeze isn't necessarily negative ("squeeze不一定都是負面的 / 正面的更多?"). The answer must acknowledge productive dimensions without dissolving the analytical force of the squeeze.

### The benefit/cost framing (not positive/negative)

The productive dimension of the squeeze is not simply a "positive side" to set against a "negative side." Such framing would dissolve the analytical force of the squeeze into a trade-off calculation — which is precisely what F2 (incommensurability) forbids. Instead, the productive dimension must be understood through the logic of **benefit/cost asymmetry of life-as-willed**.

The modern normative project, crystallized in what Uegaki (2017/2021) calls 〈意のままになる生〉 (life-as-willed) and 〈自己完結社会〉 (self-completing society), pursues the reduction of 〈意のままにならない〉 states — conditions where things do not proceed as willed. Uegaki characterizes this as the "worldview of infinite life" (〈無限の生〉) — a normative claim that a life entirely under one's control is the *ought-to-be* state of human existence. He calls this an "ideal that denies reality" (現実を否定する理想): it starts from an imagined "perfect human" and attempts to alter flesh-and-blood reality to fit this standard. The structural costs are internal to the ideal: the "Endless Hell" (無間地獄) of escalating dissatisfaction, the "Confusion of Life" (〈生の混乱〉) when uncontrollable realities are encountered, and the ultimate self-cancelling endpoint where fully achieving life-as-willed would require discarding biological humanity itself ("brain humans," 脳人間).

This project has genuine benefits: reduction of material scarcity, protection from ecological unpredictability, extension of human capabilities through technical systems. The source manuscript acknowledges these directly — none of the five dependency modes (天地 / 配分 / 貨幣 / 人工性 / 制御性) is intrinsically harmful; each arose as an adaptive response to real pressures.

But as the author argues [T1: source-ms-kaikan §2.4, citing 上柿 2021]: **the more thoroughly humans entrust all matters to control means and pursue 〈意のままになる生〉, the smaller the human role in the world's operation becomes, and control means progressively automate.** When artificiality (人工性) functions self-sufficiently without human involvement and humans become those who adapt to it rather than the reverse, that artificiality transcends its role as survival means and stands up as a new background environment — the metaverse/偽境.

The **structural costs** of life-as-willed are not merely contingent failures or side effects but necessary consequences of the inversion [T1: source-ms-kaikan §3]:
- The positive pyramid (根本性順位: 天地 > 配分 > 貨幣 > 制御性) and the inverted pyramid (生活依存構造: 制御性 > 貨幣 > 配分 > 天地) diverge further as the pursuit of 〈意のままになる生〉 intensifies
- The more thoroughly adapted to the inverted structure, the harder it becomes to return to more fundamental dependency modes
- The result is the very squeeze condition described in §1-§2: existence jointly constrained by universe and metaverse with no single optimization possible

The squeeze is, in this sense, the self-organized outcome of humanity's collective pursuit of life-as-willed — not an external imposition but an emergent consequence of humanity's participation in the progressive automation of control.

### The 〈依存転回〉 (dependency turn) as precondition of navigation [T1: source-ms-kaikan §6]

The productive dimension emerges not from the squeeze itself but from a specific *reframing* of one's relation to it. The author calls this 〈依存転回〉 (dependency turn):

When power and oppression are framed as evils that distort from outside, the subject is fixed in the position of victim. But when framed as *dependency*, the question becomes: **in what dependency structures am I participating, and how are they organized?** This reframing opens the question of how dependency modes can be reorganized (別の代謝回路の作り方 — the creation of different metabolic circuits) rather than merely suffered.

As the source manuscript notes [T1: source-ms-kaikan §6], analogous to the Buddhist 〈十二因縁〉 analysis: the chain of desire (愛) → attachment (取) → fixation of existence (有) is not severed from outside but is reorganized only through *awareness of the process itself* (連鎖のプロセスそのものへの気づき). And as Zhuangzi's 〈有所待〉 formulation cautions: there is no absolute starting point that can end all dependency chains — which is precisely why tracing and understanding the dependency chains becomes the fundamental honest path toward transformation.

The productive dimension of the squeeze is therefore not three generic "benefits" that can be listed. It is specifically the possibility of **reorganizing one's dependency modes** that opens when one recognizes oneself as a *participant in self-organized dependency structures* rather than a victim of external forces. This recognition is the precondition of navigation — and the analytical work of the thesis is what makes that recognition possible.

### Difference from the VI-3/VI-4 scaffold formulation

The three-item productive-dimension list in the prior scaffold (VI-3-VI-4: individual hybrid competencies / cultural revitalization / civilizational orientation) is a rough approximation that has now been superseded by this benefit/cost/dependency-turn analysis. The three items remain useful as **illustrative cases** of what dependency-mode reorganization looks like in practice, but they should not be presented as *constitutive* of the productive dimension.

**Do not use the VI-3-VI-4 scaffold formulation in Phase C.** Derive §VI-3 and §VI-4 prose from this document's §3 instead.

## §4 — What the squeeze is NOT (anti-metaphorical safeguards)

> **Derivation tier:** [T1: source-ms-kaikan §2.4, §6] for ecological/evolutionary framing; [T1: CLAUDE.md + viva comments] for metaphor-watering-down risk; **[T0: NLM-Uegaki-A-Sources, conv 15df9238]** for Uegaki's ecological/evolutionary framing confirmed.

The contrast in the table below represents the thesis author's positioning of Uegaki's framing relative to those traditions — not Uegaki's direct critique. Adjust §4 prose accordingly in Phase C.

To prevent the squeeze from being watered down to metaphor (the risk flagged in plan v3 §3 re: I-4), the following non-uses must be avoided in the revised thesis:

### Hard prohibitions (what the squeeze is NOT)

- **Not "pressure from technology"** — that's a metaphor. The squeeze is the structural condition F1+F2 satisfies: being between two environments whose laws are incommensurable.
- **Not "stress" or "anxiety"** — these are *experiential correlates* of the squeeze condition, not the squeeze itself. The squeeze can be fully constituted before any individual experiences psychological distress.
- **Not "trade-offs" or "tensions"** — these occur within a single environment under a common measure. F2 is precisely the absence of such a common measure. "Tension between X and Y" dissolves the squeeze's analytical force.
- **Not a moral position about technology** — the squeeze is a structural-ontological diagnosis. The thesis does not argue the metaverse should not exist; it describes the structural condition of those who must inhabit both environments.

### The key conceptual boundary: ecological/evolutionary vs. anthropological

The squeeze must be distinguished from the major existing critical-theory concepts it might superficially resemble:

| Concept | Tradition | What it names | Why the squeeze is different |
|---------|-----------|---------------|------------------------------|
| **Alienation** (Entfremdung) | Marx, Frankfurt school | Human subject's separation from species-being, product, activity, other humans | Starts from human subject and their authentic relation to labor/world. The squeeze is a between-environment structural condition prior to any subject's experience. |
| **Domination** (Herrschaft) | Frankfurt school (Horkheimer, Adorno, Marcuse) | Human subject's subjugation to the technical-rational apparatus; loss of autonomy | Names a *relation of power* between human subjects and systems. The squeeze names a *structural position between two environments* — not a power relation but an ontological condition. |
| **Enframing** (Gestell) | Heidegger | The essence of modern technology as a challenging-forth (Herausforderung) that reveals beings only as standing-reserve (Bestand) | Starts from the question of Being and its concealment; fundamentally anthropological (concerned with the human Dasein's relation to Being). The squeeze starts from the self-organization of dependency modes — a process that is indifferent to any particular human Dasein. |
| **Grammatization** | Stiegler | The discretization of continuous human flows into calculable grammes; the pharmacological logic of technical systems | Names a *mechanism internal to the metaverse's logic* — what makes the metaverse operate as it does. The squeeze names the *structural condition* of being between universe and metaverse. Grammatization is part of what makes the metaverse a distinct environment with its own laws (F2); it does not name the squeeze itself. |

**The key distinction [T1: source-ms-kaikan §2.4, §6]:** The dependency modes (天地依存 / 配分依存 / 貨幣依存 / 人工性依存 / 制御性依存) are described as "自己組織化された歴史的産物" — historically self-organized products. The squeeze is the emergent condition of these self-organizing processes reaching a stage where a second background environment (偽境) has become operationally distinct from the universe. This is an **ecological/evolutionary** description: it concerns the structural niche conditions organisms face, not the *experience* of those conditions by human subjects.

Uegaki's own framing confirms this: he treats human civilization as an "artificial ecosystem" (人為的生態系) within which the biological organism Homo is shaped. He diagnoses the modern crisis not as loss of "authentic being" but as an **ecological mismatch** — the biological human reaching "limits of adaptation" (適応の限界) to the environment it created. He rejects alienation theory's premise that technology distorts an original "pure" human nature, treating technology instead as a direct extension of human evolutionary strategy.
