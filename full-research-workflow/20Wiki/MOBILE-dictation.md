One might ask, if the Metaverse operates on a set of laws that are irreducible to the physical world or our own universe, then what are those laws exactly?

The honest answer is that we do not know yet. Even for our own universe, the laws are not fully exhausted. We first discovered Classical Newtonian laws, then we discovered quantum physics later, and there are likely more yet to be discovered.

However, there is a key difference regarding the laws of the Metaverse (perhaps we can call them the "Meta-Laws"). We cannot discover the laws of the Metaverse in the same way we discover the laws of the universe. According to the majority consensus, the laws of the universe are immutable: once discovered, they exist independently of humans and do not change.

This is not the case for the Metaverse. The laws of the Metaverse change over time, and they might even change after we thought we had finally grasped them.
