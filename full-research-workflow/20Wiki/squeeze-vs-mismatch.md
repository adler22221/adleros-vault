# Squeeze vs. Evolutionary Mismatch

> [!note] Status
> `ai-draft` — distinction worked out and author-endorsed 2026-06-02; **ready for human → canonical promotion.** Feeds [[squeeze-ontology]], the Introduction, Ch II (§0/§E), and Ch VI. Originality defense for the "squeeze" concept.

## One-line

**Evolutionary mismatch is the dyadic, diachronic, resolvable, structures-layer *special case* of the squeeze. The squeeze generalises it by adding F1 (betweenness) and F2 (principle-incommensurability). So `Squeeze₁ ≈ mismatch`, and `Squeeze₂ (the metaverse) ⊋ mismatch`.**

## The canonical definition the squeeze pushes past

> "An evolutionary mismatch can be defined as a negative consequence that results from a **trait** that evolved in one environment being placed in another environment." — Lloyd, Wilson & Sober (2011), p. 4 (emphasis in original)

> mismatch = "deviations in the environment that render **biological traits** unable… to produce their selected effects." — Cofnas (2016), p. 507
>
> "the greater the degree of mismatch between environment and design, the greater the cost… we call this the **mismatch paradigm**." — Gluckman & Hanson, *Mismatch* (2006), p. 7 (Zotero `6TGHAHHJ`); and Lieberman's "**mismatch diseases**… our Paleolithic bodies being poorly or inadequately adapted to… modern behaviors and conditions" (2013, p. 182; Zotero `WXQ5AINR`).

The canon is **trait-bound** and **change-bound** (the change temporal *or* spatial-by-migration). Both features are exactly what the squeeze generalises.

## The distinction

| | **Evolutionary mismatch** | **Squeeze** |
|---|---|---|
| **structure** | **dyadic** — a *trait* against *one* environment | **triadic** — a thing caught *between two* sides (**F1**) |
| **time** | **diachronic** — a *lag* (past-tuned trait, present env) | can be **synchronic** — co-present pressures (follows from F1) |
| **layer** | trait / **structures** ("actual") | can be **enduring-principles** ("real"), incommensurable (**F2**) |
| **resolvability** | **transient** — lag closes, or extinction | can be **standing / unresolvable** |
| **core metaphor** | **LAG** — "behind" | **BETWEENNESS** — "between" |

## The crisp form

**Mismatch is a diachronic *lag*; the squeeze is a (possibly synchronic) *betweenness*.** A mismatched organism is *behind* one environment that changed on it; a squeezed one is pressed *between* two co-present orders at once. A mismatch can have a diachronic genesis but a synchronic structure — once the metaverse exists, the world is pressed by universe **and** metaverse *simultaneously*, as a standing condition, not a lag waiting to close.

## Mapping onto the two squeezes

- **Squeeze₁ (the ancestral/biological mismatch)** = dyadic, diachronic, structures-layer, resolvable → **≈ evolutionary mismatch**. *Cite the mismatch canon here* (Gluckman & Hanson; Lieberman; Lloyd/Wilson/Sober; Laland & Brown = "adaptive lag"). It is **actual-vs-actual**: two configurations under a *shared* enduring-principle layer, hence re-fittable (adapt / move / rebuild).
- **Squeeze₂ (the metaverse)** = triadic, synchronic, principle-layer, **incommensurable (F2)**, standing → **⊋ mismatch**. It is **real-vs-real**: 道 vs 偽道, a clash *at* the enduring-principles layer with no deeper court of appeal. `tekhne→phusis` is the operation that *elevates* the made from a configuration (resolvable, mismatch-like) to an enduring principle (偽道, not).

So the **squeeze/mismatch boundary = the Squeeze₁/Squeeze₂ boundary.** This does double duty: the overlap defends the concept's empirical reality; the surplus (F1+F2) defends its novelty. See [[squeeze-ontology]] for F1–F5.

## Honesty caveats (do not over-claim)

1. The **distinctive** marks are **F1 (triadic betweenness)** and **F2 (principle-incommensurability)**. *Self-generation* ("building your own squeezer") is **NOT** squeeze-unique — niche-construction theory and "constructed/cultural mismatch" already cover self-made mismatch (e.g. Obara's ヒト/モノ ズレ *is* a self-generated mismatch; the Great Oxidation Event is a 2.4-Gyr-old one). Treat self-generation as a **shared bridge**, lead with F1/F2.
2. **Squeeze₁ genuinely *is* a mismatch.** The novelty is the *generalisation*, displayed only at Squeeze₂. Do not claim novelty in the overlap zone.
