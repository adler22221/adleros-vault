# Thesis → Book — *Between the Universe and the Metaverse*

> **Status:** Nascent / exploratory (opened 2026-06-10). The thesis is finalized and submitted; this project plans its revision into a book. Notes here are AI-drafts for Adler to review and build on iteratively.
> **Project ID:** `metaverse-book` — adjustable once a press is chosen (e.g., `metaverse-mitpress-book`).

## Goal
Revise the thesis into a **single-idea book** — the *squeeze* between the universe and the metaverse — that is at once **publicly accessible** (reaches intelligent general readers) and **scholarly reputable** (respected by the philosophy-of-technology / theory community).

## Big decisions still open (drive everything else)
1. **Book identity:** trade-crossover "big idea" book *(fewer notes, narrative drive, developmental editor)* **vs.** scholarly monograph *(fuller apparatus, peer review)*. Current lean: crossover (matches the MIT-Press register the thesis was finished in).
2. **Open access or not** — affects which presses (e.g., punctum/meson/MIT-Direct-to-Open vs conventional).
3. **Target press** — shortlist in `publishing-strategy.md`.
4. **Thesis deposit access level** — *decide BEFORE signing the 授權書*: request an **embargo / delayed open access**, not immediate open full-text (see `publishing-strategy.md` §3). This is time-sensitive at submission.
