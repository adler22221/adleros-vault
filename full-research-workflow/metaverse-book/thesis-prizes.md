## ⏰ ACT NOW — two time-sensitive, high-fit opportunities

### 1. Berggruen Prize Essay Competition — **DEADLINE 17 Aug 2026, 23:59 PT (confirmed on live page)**
- **The headline opportunity.** Open to all (so MA-eligible). Cross-cultural / "great transformations" philosophy — your East–West tech-philosophy is squarely in scope.
- **Value:** **US$50,000 for the best English essay AND US$50,000 for the best Chinese essay** (two tracks); winners **published** by BI Press / *Noema*. Word limits ~**10,000 (English) / 15,000 (Chinese)**.
- **Bilingual = ideal for you** — you could enter the Chinese track, the English track, or adapt different material to each.
- **Action:** check the **2026 prompt/theme** and rules at https://berggruen.org/essay-competition-open ; adapt the *squeeze* argument (or a chapter) into a standalone essay within the word limit. ~2 months out — this is the one to move on first.
- Confidence: **high** (deadline + value verified on the live page).

### 2. 南海碩士論文獎 / Taiwan Philosophical Association Master's Thesis Award (台灣哲學學會碩士論文獎)
- **The best dedicated THESIS prize for you** (submit the thesis itself, not an essay).
- **Eligibility (verified verbatim):** any ROC-university **master's** graduate; thesis must be "philosophical in character" but **NOT limited to philosophy-department theses**; **Chinese OR English**; thesis completed within the **3 academic years** before the deadline → your mid-2026 thesis qualifies for the 2026–2028 cycles. Topic (comparative philosophy of technology) is in scope.
- **Value:** 4 awards/yr — 傑出獎 NT$20,000 · 優秀獎 NT$15,000 · 佳作獎 NT$10,000 ×2 + bilingual certificate. (No publication bundled.) Winners **present at the TPA annual conference**.
- **Deadline:** 2025 cycle closed **30 Aug 2025**; **2026 call not yet posted** as of June 2026 — expect **~late Aug 2026**. Submit full-text PDF + graduation certificate by email (2025 address: tpa.staff1@gmail.com; subject "南海碩士論文獎：論文甄選").
- **Action:** watch https://tpatw.org/events/nan-awards/ + the TPA Facebook, and **ask the 系辦** for the exact 2026 date (the site returns 403 to bots; the dept will know). Confidence: **high** on eligibility/value, **date unconfirmed**.
