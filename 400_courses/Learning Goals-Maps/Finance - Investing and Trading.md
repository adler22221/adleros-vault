1.  Start by learning the basics of finance and economics. You can take online courses or read books on these topics.

3.  [Learn about nonlinear mathematical models such as artificial neural networks, support vector machines, genetic algorithms, wavelet analysis, and empirical model analysis](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-642-00742-2_54)[1](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-642-00742-2_54). These models are based on complexity science-informed financial theories and models.
