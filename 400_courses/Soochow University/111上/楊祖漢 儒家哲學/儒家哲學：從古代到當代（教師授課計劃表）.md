**三、教學目標 Objectives** 儒學在當代有長足的發展，本課程希望通過當代新儒家唐君毅、牟宗三兩位有關儒學的重要見解，論述儒學在古代及當代的發展。又根據當代儒學的觀點，論述韓國朝鮮朝儒學的重要論爭。 Confucianism has made great progress in contemporary times. This course hopes to discuss the development of Confucianism in ancient and contemporary times through the important views of Tang Junyi and Mou Zongsan. According to the viewpoint of contemporary Confucianism, I'm going to discuss the important controversy of Korean Confucianism. **四、課程內容 Course Description** **●**整體敘述 Overall Description
