## Problems with using modern science to "verify" East Asian scientific theories
TCM as example:
- while the efficacy of Chinese medicine can be evaluated with modern scientific methods, such approach reduces TCM practices and herbs to mere tools/components for practices under the "modern science" rationale
- consequence: decontextualized from the way the system is developed/inferred
	- RCT is incompatible with the TCM's view on human health, as the same symptoms arise from different contexts, thus the treatment will only be effective if the individual's unique context is taken into account
