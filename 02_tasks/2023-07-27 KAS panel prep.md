Different kinds of capitals

If solely aware, thus solely dependent on one kind of capital (reducing all kinds of capitals to economic capital, or reducing all kinds of social relations to transactional relations), that creates a fragile structure— this is why unemployment is a huge problem , or why not having a lot of money is a huge problem 

Otherwise, many people are not employed in the traditional sense, but are creating immense value to the society 

At the micro level, housewives or househusbands, volunteers 
Macro level, nonprofits

Employment is not the problem per se
Employment is only one of the many forms or facets of interdependence among society members 

It’s more precise to say that AI forces is to rethink about our interdependence 
And the key to mutual flourishing or conviviality, is to always keep “healthy interdependence” in mind and find a most suitable way to achieve it
You might self-sustain your life by farming 
Live upon inheritance
