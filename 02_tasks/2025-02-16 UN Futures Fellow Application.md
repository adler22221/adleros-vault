# Thematic Fellows

We're looking for someone who can communicate and mobilize effectively. Fellows will:

1. Serve as a prominent advocate for youth involvement in global governance, integrating young people’s voices and perspectives into policy discussions and international forums.
    
2. Organize and facilitate consultations, events, and campaigns that empower young leaders to advocate for their needs and interests in the context of the SDGs.
    
3. Lead youth mobilization efforts in preparation for the SDG Summit 2027, ensuring that young people’s perspectives and priorities are prominently represented in discussions and outcomes.

# Experience in the Thematic Area

Each Thematic Fellow will lead their assigned Action Group, championing their specific thematic focus and acting as the focal point for campaigns, collaborations, and outputs. Fellows will ensure that youth-led priorities and solutions within their thematic area are represented at all levels of decision-making, driving transformative action in alignment with the SDGs and the [2100 Roadmap](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1-qVT8ghXnYcJcU65m59uJPVTBvQt9PJq/view).

**👥 People**

_Focuses on people-centered strategies for 2100, **addressing demographic shifts' impact on healthcare, education, and employment**. It emphasizes youth well-being within aging populations and explores migration patterns._

**🌍 Planet**

_Concentrated on **new models to empower young people to lead in climate** adaptation and mitigation, promote green practices among young entrepreneurs, and advocate for sustainable policies for the benefit of all generations._  

**🖖 Prosperity**

_Explores **economic trends, entrepreneurship, and economic empowerment, focusing on the most marginalized.** It addresses the impact of new technologies, microfinance, and youth-led innovation to foster economic growth and equality._
