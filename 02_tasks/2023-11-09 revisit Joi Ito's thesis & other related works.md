Dear 伊藤先生, Mikaさん & Daumさん

I just landed in Haneda and I’m very excited about our meetup!

There’s one very recent development that I’d like to bring to our conversation tomorrow: 

Without a bachelor’s degree, I was offered the opportunity to join the graduate program at the Department of Philosophy, Soochow University in 2021 by the Dean of its School of Social Sciences and Humanities, philosopher Dr. Chienkuo Mi, to bring educational innovation to the department and produce truly socially relevant philosophical research. While I had no background and literacy in computer science and digital technology, Dr. Mi valued my transdisciplinary background and self-education and challenged me to take “metaverse” as the subject of my Master’s research.

After two years, I have completed my credit requirements for my Masters degree. With my immersion in communities such as Web 3 for all, along with relevant literature, I came up with a research plan that both Dr. Mi and I just realized is beyond the scope of a Master’s thesis and deserve to be iterated and developed fully.

As the Department of Philosophy at Soochow University doesn’t have a Doctorate program, in the past few days we began to discuss the possibility of bringing this plan to somewhere that is willing to take it as a doctoral research project.
