many have realized the inherent, inevitable inequality that follows the inequal adoption/utilization of technology
but a lot of them chose an "anti-technology" approach -- back to the good old times
but this renders people into another type of dependency / 有待
dependency on the specific constellation of specific things
and such dependency reduces adaptability

which ultimately, is the same with what these people critique:
the dependency on specific technology 反噬 reverts those who are dependent into inadaptive beings -- whether in terms fetishism of technology or under its domination, and this is evidenced by the disadvantage of those who are fixated to obsolete forms of artificiality;
and the inequality also arise from such inadaptability, as those who can adopt and utilize new technology gain an advantage, while those who cannot become disadvantaged

so the key here, is not the abolishment of technology, nor the acceleration of technology (adoption)
but to decrease "medium-dependence"
empower people to thrive/flourish no matter what medium is (not) at their disposal
and restrict artificiality from generating and imposing dependency on people
In 莊子’s words, to ensure people are on their way to 無待
