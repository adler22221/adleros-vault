**HUANG Kuan-Min**  
Institute of Chinese Literature and Philosophy, Academia Sinica  
  
  
  
  
**SESSION 8 :**  
  
**Planetary Art, dwelling, and vitality**  
   
  
  
  
**Abstract:**  
   
In this session, I intend to raise some questions implicated in the idea of planetary art, which are not always evident. Moreover, there could be some hidden conflicts. But as art does not avoid conflict and is often engaged in negotiation, it might be useful to start with the remarks of certain ideas to find own’s way of approach. The following are some guidelines for my presentation.  
(1) Eco-geo-aesthetics: The main concern begins with the spatial (geological and geographic) condition for dwelling (oikos as home). The aesthetics reveal the existence of body (in mineral, vegetal, animal forms). How the body acts, in a conditioned way and within a mutual recognition framework.   
(2) Scale: The scale of planet is at the same time extensive and intensive. Not only the quantity, but also the quality is concerned. The planet provides a view of the universe (kosmos). Macro-cosmos and micro-cosmos are not two verbal abstract terms nor even two opposites, they constitute a certain continuum in which the spectrum of vitality is full of disparate distribution.  
(3) Engagement and Being-In-place: Based on the aesthetic and bodily conditions, from human beings to diverse species, the capability to affecting and to be affected determines different scales of response (movement, moving, inhabitation) with certain rhythms. The seasonal and temporal factors are implied in this dimension.   
(4) Skill and technics: Even insects have their own specific skills of dwelling. The art of probing roots for plants are a constant for surviving. Through the lens of technology, especially in the era of artificiality (artificial intelligence or life, virtuality), the task is not just to find out some similarities on the basis of imitation or simulation, but to realize the real embodiment out of the requirement of vitality, not to escape the fatality or mortality, but to discover all kinds of endurance in transformation.
